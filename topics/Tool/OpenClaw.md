OpenClaw is an open-source personal AI assistant platform designed to act as a versatile, hackable, and extensible tool for automating tasks across digital environments. It operates locally or in the cloud, integrates with multiple chat applications (e.g., WhatsApp, Telegram, Discord), and provides persistent memory, browser control, and full system access. Users can extend its functionality through custom skills or plugins, making it adaptable for personal, professional, or team use cases. OpenClaw emphasizes user control, privacy, and community-driven development.
