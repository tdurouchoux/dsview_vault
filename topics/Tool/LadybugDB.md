LadybugDB is an embedded, columnar graph database designed specifically for highly regulated industries. It is built to support agentic AI applications, offering high-performance analytical queries on graph data. Key features include zero external dependencies, serverless operation, and compatibility with edge, embedded, or cloud environments. LadybugDB emphasizes security, reliability, and enterprise support, making it a viable successor to Kuzu, which was discontinued after its acquisition by Apple.
