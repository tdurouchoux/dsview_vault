GeoUtil is an all-in-one online geography toolkit designed for working with maps, coordinates, and geographic data. It offers a variety of tools for measuring distances, converting geographic data formats, extracting coordinates, and more. The toolkit is entirely browser-based, ensuring that user data never leaves their device, thus maintaining privacy and security. GeoUtil supports various open standards such as GeoJSON, TopoJSON, Shapefile, KML, WKT, and CSV, making it a versatile tool for GIS professionals, developers, and geography enthusiasts.
