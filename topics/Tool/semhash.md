Semhash is a semantic deduplication and dataset multitool designed to efficiently identify and remove semantically similar documents from large datasets. It improves the quality of data used for training machine learning models, particularly in scenarios where overlap between training and testing sets can lead to overfitting and inflated performance metrics. Semhash operates rapidly on large datasets, allowing for fast deduplication processes, which is especially important given the growing focus on quality over quantity in training data.
