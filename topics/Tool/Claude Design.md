Claude Design is a visual design prototyping tool developed by Anthropic. It leverages AI to assist users in creating dashboards and visual analytics prototypes quickly and efficiently. The tool incorporates structured elicitation steps to gather user requirements, such as audience, key questions, and time scope, before generating visual outputs. While it can produce polished and professional-looking dashboards, its effectiveness depends on the quality of the input prompts and the user's understanding of dashboard design fundamentals.
