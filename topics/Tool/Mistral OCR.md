Mistral OCR is an Optical Character Recognition API designed for advanced document understanding. It excels in processing complex documents, including those with interleaved imagery, tables, mathematical expressions, and intricate layouts. By taking images and PDFs as input, it can extract structured content and provide a high level of accuracy and cognitive comprehension, making it suitable for various applications beyond simple text extraction.
