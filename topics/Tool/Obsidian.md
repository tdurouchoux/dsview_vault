Obsidian is a popular note-taking and personal knowledge management (PKM) software that operates on a local-first, markdown-based system. It allows users to create, link, and organize notes in a graph structure, enabling seamless navigation and visualization of relationships between different pieces of information. Obsidian supports plugins, such as Dataview and Marp, to enhance functionality, and it is widely used for building and maintaining structured knowledge bases.
