Datawrapper is a web-based tool designed for creating interactive and visually appealing charts, maps, and tables. It focuses on accessibility and ease of use, enabling users to design data visualizations without requiring extensive programming knowledge. Datawrapper provides various customization options, including themes and text adjustments, targeting the needs of journalists, researchers, and data communicators.
