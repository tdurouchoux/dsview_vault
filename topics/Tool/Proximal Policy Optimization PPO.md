Proximal Policy Optimization (PPO) is a popular reinforcement learning algorithm used for training complex models, particularly in environments where the model interacts with human feedback. PPO is known for its stability and reliability, as it applies constraints to the policy updates to avoid drastic changes in model behavior. This algorithm is especially useful in the context of RLHF, where fine-tuning a pre-trained language model includes balancing exploration of new outputs with maintaining the coherence of the trained model.
