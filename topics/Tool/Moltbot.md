Moltbot is an open-source personal AI assistant that can perform a wide range of tasks such as managing emails, scheduling, and automating various processes. It operates on the user's own machine, ensuring data privacy and control. Moltbot can be integrated with multiple chat applications like WhatsApp, Telegram, and Discord, making it highly accessible. It is designed to be hackable and customizable, allowing users to extend its functionality through community-built skills or by creating their own.
