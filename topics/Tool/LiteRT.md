LiteRT is Google's on-device framework for high-performance machine learning (ML) and generative AI (GenAI) deployment on edge platforms. It focuses on efficient conversion, runtime, and optimization, making on-device ML inference easier and more efficient. LiteRT supports advanced GPU/NPU acceleration and offers superior performance for ML and GenAI tasks. It is designed for cross-platform deployment on a wide range of hardware, including Android, iOS, Linux, macOS, Windows, Web, and IoT devices.
