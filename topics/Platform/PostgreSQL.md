PostgreSQL, also known as Postgres, is a powerful, open-source object-relational database management system (ORDBMS) renowned for its extensibility, scalability, and compliance with SQL standards. It supports advanced data types, indexing, full-text search, transactional integrity, concurrent editing, and crash recovery. PostgreSQL is widely used in AI and data pipelines as a sink for structured data, enabling efficient storage, querying, and retrieval of processed information. Its features include support for schemas, primary keys, foreign keys, constraints, nested transactions, materialized views, table inheritance, and asynchronous replication.
