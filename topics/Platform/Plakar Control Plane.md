Plakar Control Plane is a self-hosted backup management platform built on top of the open-source Plakar engine. It provides organization-wide visibility and control over backup policies, sources, storage, schedules, retention, replication, and verification. The Control Plane is designed to be deployed on infrastructure owned by the user, ensuring data sovereignty and compliance with local regulations. It offers a free plan and integrates with various storage backends like S3-compatible systems.
