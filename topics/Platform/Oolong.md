Oolong is a benchmark designed to evaluate long-context reasoning and aggregation capabilities in language models. It measures a model's ability to analyze multiple local chunks of information and synthesize them into a global answer, often requiring the model to navigate and aggregate information across inputs that exceed its native context window. RLMs have demonstrated state-of-the-art performance on Oolong, highlighting their superiority in handling long-context tasks.
