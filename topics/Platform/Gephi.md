Gephi is an open-source platform primarily used for visualizing and analyzing large networks and complex systems. It provides a comprehensive suite of tools for importing graph data, running layout and ranking algorithms, and customizing the visual representation of networks. Gephi supports a wide range of plugins to extend its functionality and is widely used in domains like data science, digital humanities, and social network analysis. The platform is designed to handle large datasets efficiently and offers features for dynamic exploration and interaction with network data.
