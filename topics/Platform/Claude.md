Claude is a series of powerful AI models developed by Anthropic, including Claude 4, designed for natural language understanding and generation. The models are capable of following explicit instructions, handling complex multi-step reasoning, and executing parallel tasks efficiently. Built to enhance user interactions through prompt engineering, they offer features like Contextual Retrieval and prompt caching, facilitating faster and more cost-effective retrieval from large knowledge bases.
