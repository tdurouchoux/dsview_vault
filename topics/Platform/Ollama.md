Ollama is a platform that provides users with the ability to run and interact with large language models (LLMs) directly on their computers. It offers local execution capabilities while ensuring privacy by avoiding external data transmission and supports GGUF (Generalized General Use Format) models available on the Hugging Face Hub. Ollama enhances privacy, control, and performance for AI operations, similar to other tools like Groq.
