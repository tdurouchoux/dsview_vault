Ollama is a platform that enables users to run and interact with large language models (LLMs) directly on their computers, providing local AI processing capabilities for various models. It supports GGUF (Generalized General Use Format) models available on the Hugging Face Hub, offering tools for embedding and generating text while enhancing privacy, control, and performance for AI operations.
