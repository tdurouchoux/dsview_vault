Doctrine is a legal AI platform designed to assist lawyers and legal professionals by automating repetitive and time-consuming tasks such as case analysis, legal research, and document drafting. It aims to enhance legal productivity and security by leveraging extensive legal databases and advanced AI capabilities. The platform includes features like Doctrine Chatbot for legal research, Doctrine Flow for managing litigation cases, and Jobexit for simulating contract terminations. It also emphasizes data security and compliance with regulations like the RGPD.
