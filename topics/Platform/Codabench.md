Codabench is an open-source platform designed for organizing AI benchmarks and competitions. It provides a flexible and user-friendly interface for defining tasks, such as datasets and metrics, and supports both traditional and inverted benchmarks where participants submit algorithms or datasets, respectively. The platform enables real-time feedback, detailed results, and leaderboards for participants. Organizers can customize benchmarks extensively, including the scoring program and compute workers, allowing for scalable and reproducible evaluations. Codabench is built as a successor to CodaLab, incorporating advancements and community feedback to enhance its capabilities.
