Figma is a collaborative design platform that enables users to create, prototype, and share interactive designs in real-time. It supports a wide range of design workflows, including UI/UX design, wireframing, and prototyping. Figma allows teams to work together seamlessly, providing tools for annotation, iteration, and exploration of design ideas. Its canvas-based interface makes it ideal for visual collaboration, enabling users to organize, duplicate, refine, and share design artifacts.
