Neptune.ai is a machine learning experiment tracking and metadata management platform designed to help data science and ML engineering teams manage their model training processes. It provides a centralized interface for logging, storing, and organizing model-related metadata, enabling teams to compare experimental results, monitor model performance, and facilitate collaboration. Neptune.ai is particularly useful for teams conducting extensive experiments and requires detailed tracking of various parameters and model outputs.
