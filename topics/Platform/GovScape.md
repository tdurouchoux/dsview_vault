GovScape is a public search system designed to facilitate multimodal searches across a vast collection of government PDFs. It supports various search functionalities, including metadata filtering, exact text search, semantic text search, and visual search. The system is built to enhance access and discoverability of government documents, making it easier for users to find specific information within millions of PDF pages. GovScape's architecture and open-source codebase are designed to be scalable, with the potential to handle searches across over 100 million PDFs.
