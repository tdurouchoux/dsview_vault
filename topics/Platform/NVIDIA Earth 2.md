NVIDIA Earth-2 is a comprehensive family of open models, libraries, and frameworks designed for global weather and climate AI. It provides a fully open software stack that accelerates every stage of forecasting, from data processing to high-resolution visualization. The platform enables researchers, startups, and government agencies to run, fine-tune, and deploy sophisticated forecasting systems on their own infrastructure with unprecedented speed and cost-efficiency. It shifts from resource-intensive physics models to an end-to-end AI pipeline, making professional-grade weather AI accessible worldwide.
