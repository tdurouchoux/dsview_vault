Tensor Processing Units (TPUs) are specialized hardware accelerators developed by Google for machine learning tasks. They are designed to accelerate the performance of deep learning models, particularly in training and inference scenarios. In the context of GameNGen, a TPU is utilized to achieve real-time simulation at rates like 20 frames per second, demonstrating the efficiency and speed advantages of using such hardware for complex computations in neural models.
