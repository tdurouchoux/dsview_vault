SaaS is a cloud-based service where applications are hosted remotely and made available to users over the internet. This model eliminates the need for local installation and maintenance of software, allowing users to access applications anytime and anywhere. SaaS products typically include a monthly or yearly subscription fee and are widely used for data analytics, CRM, and various business applications, although they come with challenges regarding feature development and user feedback.
