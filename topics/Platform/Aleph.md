Aleph is a powerful search engine that is designed to store and manage complex data sets, particularly in investigative contexts. It operates on data expressed in FollowTheMoney (FtM) entities and provides advanced capabilities for searching, viewing, and manipulating these entities. It supports higher-level data organization concepts such as datasets and access control, making it a critical tool for researchers dealing with large volumes of interconnected data.
