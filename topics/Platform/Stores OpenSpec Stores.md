Stores, in the context of OpenSpec, refer to a beta feature that allows teams to plan and manage specifications (specs) in a centralized, shared repository. This enables cross-repo collaboration, where requirements and plans are owned by one team and consumed by others, ensuring consistency and reducing misalignment. Stores act as a single source of truth for both human developers and AI coding assistants, facilitating scalable and organized development workflows.
