Document Layout Analysis, also referred to as Layout Analysis, is the process of identifying and understanding the structure and arrangement of elements within a document. This includes detecting and categorizing regions such as text blocks, tables, figures, headings, titles, and headers. It is a critical step in OCR pipelines and document processing tasks, enabling models to accurately parse and interpret complex document layouts. Techniques like PP-DocLayout-V3 are often employed to enhance the accuracy and context-awareness of information extraction.
