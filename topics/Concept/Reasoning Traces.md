Reasoning traces refer to the internal sequences of thoughts and actions generated by models during problem-solving tasks. Analyzing these traces provides insights into how models think, their decision-making processes, and the intermediate steps taken towards arriving at final outcomes. This concept emphasizes the importance of understanding not just the correctness of answers provided by models, but also the quality and effectiveness of the reasoning that led to those answers.
