Artificial General Intelligence (AGI) refers to the theoretical and hypothetical ability of a machine to understand, learn, and apply intelligence across a broad range of tasks, comparable to human cognitive capabilities. AGI represents the next frontier in AI research, focusing on developing systems that can autonomously reason, adapt, and perform effectively in an open-ended environment. Research in this field involves advancements in machine learning algorithms, cognitive architectures, and understanding human thought processes, while also addressing important challenges related to reliability, ethics, and alignment with human values.
