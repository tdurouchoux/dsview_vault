Reward function engineering is the process of designing and refining reward functions that guide the behavior of machine learning models, particularly in reinforcement learning frameworks. This involves defining how the success of an agent is measured based on its actions and their outcomes in a given environment. The concept emphasizes the importance of aligning reward functions with long-term goals and ensuring they reflect the true metrics of success in real-world applications.
