Long-Horizon Chain-of-Thought (LongCoT) is a benchmark designed to evaluate a model's ability to perform complex, multi-step reasoning over extended sequences of intermediate steps. Unlike traditional chain-of-thought prompting, LongCoT requires models to maintain coherence and accuracy over long reasoning chains, often involving hundreds or thousands of steps. RLMs have demonstrated state-of-the-art performance on LongCoT, achieving significant improvements over vanilla LLMs and highlighting their potential as a paradigm for advanced reasoning.
