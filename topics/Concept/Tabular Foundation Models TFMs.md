A class of machine learning models, specifically pre-trained deep learning models, designed for tabular data tasks. These models leverage large-scale pretraining on diverse tabular datasets or synthetic data to enable in-context learning, transfer learning, and strong performance, particularly on smaller datasets where traditional methods may struggle. They often utilize transformer architectures and principles like Prior-Data Fitted Networks (PFNs). Examples include TabPFN, TabICL, and Mitra, though their practical applicability may be constrained by data size limitations and computational costs.
