Reproducibility, also referred to as reproducible research, is a fundamental principle in scientific and computational research, particularly in fields like MLOps and data science. It refers to the ability to recreate or replicate experiments, analyses, and results using the same data, methods, and conditions as the original work. This ensures transparency, reliability, and trustworthiness in scientific and data science projects by allowing others to verify, build upon, or reuse the findings. It involves detailed documentation, version control, open-access data, standardized methodologies, and the use of tools and platforms like CodaLab and Codabench to facilitate reproducibility.
