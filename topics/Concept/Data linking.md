Data linking refers to the process of identifying and connecting records that correspond to the same entity across different datasets. This is crucial for eliminating duplicate records and enhancing data quality, especially in large organizations where the same entity may be represented in multiple disparate systems without a unique identifier. The process utilizes algorithms and methods to match records based on similar attributes, often requiring techniques like fuzzy matching to accommodate variations in data.
