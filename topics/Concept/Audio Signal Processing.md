Audio Signal Processing encompasses a range of techniques and algorithms used to manipulate or analyze audio signals. This field includes methods like filtering, spectral analysis, and sound synthesis, which can be applied in various contexts such as music production, telecommunications, and acoustic analysis. The complexities arise mainly due to the variance in sound characteristics and the perceptual aspects of audio that need to be handled effectively.
