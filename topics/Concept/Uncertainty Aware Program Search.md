Uncertainty-aware program search is an advanced reasoning technique that incorporates uncertainty estimation into the search process for executable programs or solutions. By evaluating the confidence of intermediate steps or subproblems, the model can dynamically adjust its decomposition strategy, prioritize high-certainty paths, and avoid overthinking or unnecessary recursion. This approach has been shown to improve performance in long-context and long-reasoning tasks, particularly in the context of RLMs and related paradigms.
