Supervised Fine-Tuning (SFT) is a machine learning technique where pre-trained models are further trained on labeled datasets to enhance their performance on specific tasks. This method helps adapt the model to a specific task using smaller, task-specific datasets, refining their ability to generate accurate and contextually relevant responses, follow instructions, or answer questions in a more user-friendly manner.
