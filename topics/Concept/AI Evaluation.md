AI Evaluation refers to the systematic and interdisciplinary assessment of AI models or systems to determine their performance, reliability, safety, robustness, and suitability for specific tasks. This involves designing metrics, benchmarks, testing datasets, validation processes, and protocols (such as development evaluations, assurance evaluations, external evaluations, and red teaming) to ensure that AI systems behave as expected in real-world scenarios. Evaluations are critical for identifying failures, biases, risks, and areas for improvement in AI systems, particularly in the context of large language models (LLMs) and other advanced AI tools.
