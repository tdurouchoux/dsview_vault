This concept refers to the process of recording the actions and states of a reinforcement learning agent as it interacts with an environment, in order to create a rich training dataset. In the development of GameNGen, a dedicated RL agent plays DOOM to generate trajectories that serve as the basis for training the generative model. This method is essential for capturing diverse gameplay scenarios that contribute to the model's overall performance.
