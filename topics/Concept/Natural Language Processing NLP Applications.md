NLP Applications encompass a wide range of tasks and functions that deal with the interaction between computers and human language. This includes activities such as sentiment analysis, machine translation, information extraction, and question answering. In recent years, integrating knowledge graphs into NLP applications has enhanced the systems' ability to understand context, semantic relationships, and implied meanings, providing a richer and more accurate user experience.
