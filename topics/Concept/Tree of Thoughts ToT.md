Tree of Thoughts (ToT) is a prompting strategy designed to model the reasoning process of a language model as a tree structure, enabling exploration of various paths based on intermediate reasoning steps. This method allows for backtracking to prior nodes if specific paths lead to less promising outcomes, thereby facilitating more comprehensive exploration and selection of thoughts. ToT helps enhance the capabilities of LLMs by providing structured reasoning but may impose limitations due to the rigid tree architecture.
