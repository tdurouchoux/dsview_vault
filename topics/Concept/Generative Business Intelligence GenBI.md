Generative Business Intelligence (GenBI) leverages AI to transform how business users interact with data, allowing for rapid dashboard creation and analysis through natural language interfaces. It involves a programmatic approach to Business Intelligence, enabling the automation of dashboard generation and metrics definition, thereby reducing the technical barrier for users who may lack extensive data skills. GenBI incorporates principles from the declarative data stack and emphasizes semantic understanding in analytics.
