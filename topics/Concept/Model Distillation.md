Model distillation or Knowledge Distillation is an optimization technique in machine learning where a smaller model (the student) is trained to replicate the performance and behavior of a larger, pre-trained model (the teacher). This method is employed to create efficient models that maintain much of the performance characteristics of their larger counterparts while requiring less computational resources, making it particularly useful for low-latency inference.
