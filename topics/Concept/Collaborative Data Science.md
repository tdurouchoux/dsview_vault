Collaborative data science involves teams of researchers, data scientists, engineers, and other stakeholders working together to solve complex problems. It emphasizes teamwork, shared resources, and interdisciplinary approaches to leverage diverse expertise and perspectives. This includes collaboration in MLOps, where teams work collaboratively on data, models, and code, aiming to reduce domain silos and integrate insights from various stages of the ML lifecycle. Tools, platforms, and community-driven initiatives facilitate collaboration, ensuring that projects are inclusive, efficient, and impactful.
