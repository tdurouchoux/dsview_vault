Architectural Knowledge Management (AKM) is a systematic approach to capturing, storing, sharing, and reusing architectural knowledge within an organization. This includes documenting decisions, their rationale, and the context in which they were made. AKM aims to improve software quality, maintainability, and collaboration by ensuring that architectural knowledge is not lost over time and is accessible to all team members. ADRs are a key component of AKM.
