Agent-based knowledge curation involves using AI agents to automate the process of reading, summarizing, cross-referencing, and maintaining a knowledge base. These agents can handle repetitive tasks such as updating pages, detecting contradictions, and ensuring consistency, allowing humans to focus on higher-level tasks like curating sources and asking strategic questions. This approach leverages the scalability and speed of AI while relying on human judgment for validation and direction.
