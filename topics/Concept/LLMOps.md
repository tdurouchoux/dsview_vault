LLMOps, or Large Language Model Operations, refers to the practices aimed at managing the lifecycle and performance of large language models in production environments. Similar to DevOps, LLMOps focuses on monitoring model performance, enabling continuous improvement through logging and data annotation, and ensuring the efficient deployment and scaling of LLM applications. It encompasses strategies and tools that facilitate tracking and optimizing the operational aspects of LLMs.
