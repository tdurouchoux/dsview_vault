The AI Index Report is an annual publication by Stanford University's Institute for Human-Centered Artificial Intelligence (HAI). It tracks, collates, distills, and visualizes data related to artificial intelligence (AI) to provide unbiased, rigorously vetted, and broadly sourced insights. The report aims to help policymakers, researchers, executives, journalists, and the public understand the complex field of AI, including its technical progress, economic influence, and societal impact. It covers various aspects such as AI capabilities, investment, adoption, education, policy, and public opinion.
