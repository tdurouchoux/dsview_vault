Latent space refers to a representation of compressed high-dimensional data where similar data points are located close to each other. It is used in machine learning and data science for analyzing and visualizing complex datasets through methods like clustering and embedding. In the context of diffusion models, processing occurs in this latent space to facilitate efficient computations during the generation process, enabling models to learn and interpolate between data points more effectively.
