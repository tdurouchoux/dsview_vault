Synthetic data refers to artificially generated data that mimics the statistical properties of real-world data. In the context of RLHF, synthetic data may be used to augment training datasets, especially when real data is scarce or costly to obtain, enabling more robust model training and evaluation. The generation of synthetic data helps to overcome limitations such as data scarcity or privacy concerns, improves model robustness, and allows researchers to simulate scenarios for evaluating model performance without compromising privacy or security.
