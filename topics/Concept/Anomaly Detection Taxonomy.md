Anomaly Detection Taxonomy is a structured classification of the various methodologies and algorithms used in detecting anomalies in datasets. It encompasses different categories based on characteristics such as data type (univariate vs multivariate), approach (distance-based, density-based, predictive models), and context (point, contextual, collective anomalies), allowing researchers and practitioners to identify the most suitable techniques for specific applications.
