A Document Parsing Benchmark is a standardized framework designed to evaluate the performance of document parsing tools, particularly their ability to convert complex documents like PDFs into structured, machine-readable formats. This benchmark assesses how well the parsed output preserves the original document's structure, content, and meaning, ensuring reliability for downstream tasks such as AI agent decision-making. It typically includes diverse datasets, metrics, and evaluation dimensions to measure accuracy, fidelity, and usability in real-world scenarios.
