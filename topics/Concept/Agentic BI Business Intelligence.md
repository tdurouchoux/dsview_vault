Agentic BI refers to a next-generation approach to business intelligence that incorporates autonomous agents to interpret, analyze, and act on data. These agents, powered by AI and machine learning, can automate decision-making processes, provide contextual insights, and execute workflows based on data trends or user requests. Agentic BI platforms like Looker aim to move beyond static reporting by enabling proactive, AI-driven data exploration and governance.
