Trust in AI systems refers to the confidence users have in automated systems and their outputs. It encompasses the understanding of how these systems operate, their limitations, and their potential for error. As AI technologies, particularly generative models like LLMs, evolve, they invoke new considerations regarding trustworthiness, as they can produce outputs that are persuasive and confident but may not always be correct or reliable. Ensuring that both creators and users understand the degree of trust necessary for various applications is crucial in fostering responsible AI use.
