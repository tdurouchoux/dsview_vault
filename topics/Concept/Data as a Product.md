The concept of treating data as a reusable, well-defined, and intentionally designed product that delivers value to consumers within an organization. Data products are curated, domain-specific collections of data assets that are discoverable, accessible, and governed. They are designed to be self-serve, enabling teams to treat data with the same care as consumer-facing products. This involves deliberate lifecycle management, including innovation, maintenance, and retirement, with clear ownership to ensure accuracy, reliability, and compliance. Data products improve collaboration, efficiency, and trust in data-driven decision-making by adhering to standards and best practices.
