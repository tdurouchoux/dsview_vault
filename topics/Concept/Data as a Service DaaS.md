Data as a Service (DaaS) is a model where data is provided to users on demand, allowing organizations to leverage data resources effectively. It is relevant in the context of data teams being seen as service organizations that provide essential data, tools, and analyses to facilitate informed decision-making across different business functions. In this model, the data team acts as an intermediary that ensures other departments access reliable and timely data, thus fostering collaboration and driving strategic initiatives.
