Faithfulness in AI models relates to the accuracy and reliability with which an AI exposes its reasoning process in the context of its outputs. Specifically, it involves the extent to which a model's explanations, such as its Chain-of-Thought, genuinely reflect the cognitive pathways that led to its decisions. This concept is vital for establishing trust in AI systems, particularly in applications where understanding the basis of model decisions is crucial for safety and ethical adherence.
