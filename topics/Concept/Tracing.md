Tracing in the context of LLM applications involves capturing detailed logs of every step in the execution of an AI system, including LLM calls, tool invocations, and retrieval steps. These traces are hierarchical and provide insights into the flow of data and decisions within the application. Tracing is essential for debugging, performance optimization, and ensuring transparency in AI-driven workflows. Platforms like Langfuse enable asynchronous tracing, which ensures that the tracing process does not block the application, allowing for efficient and scalable monitoring.
