A multi-modal data catalog is a unified system that manages and governs diverse data assets, including structured, semi-structured, and unstructured data, as well as AI models. Unlike traditional catalogs that focus solely on tabular data, multi-modal catalogs provide a holistic view of all data and AI assets, enabling better governance, discovery, and collaboration across an organization. These catalogs support multiple data formats and engines, ensuring flexibility and interoperability.
