Persistent Memory refers to the capability of an AI system to retain and recall user-specific context, preferences, and historical interactions over extended periods. Unlike short-term memory used in typical chat interactions, persistent memory allows AI agents to provide personalized responses, maintain continuity in conversations, and adapt to user needs dynamically. This feature is critical for building trust and efficiency in personal AI assistants, as it enables them to act as long-term collaborators rather than one-off tools.
