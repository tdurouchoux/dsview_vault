Governance-as-a-Service is an approach where a specialized team provides governance support to various organizational units, facilitating the implementation of data contracts and governance practices. This model emphasizes flexibility and efficiency, enabling teams to focus on their core responsibilities while ensuring that governance principles are seamlessly integrated into workflows. Governance-as-a-Service aims to make compliance with data governance easier and to enhance collaboration among different teams.
