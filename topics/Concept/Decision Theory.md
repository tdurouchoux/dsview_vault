Decision Theory is a framework for making choices under uncertainty, integrating statistical methods with economic principles and psychological aspects of decision-making. It aims to identify the optimal decision based on the potential outcomes and their probabilities, often incorporating expected utility and cost-benefit analysis. In the context of data science, it emphasizes using statistical learning methods to estimate potential risks and rewards of different actions—an approach particularly favored in Bayesian analysis.
