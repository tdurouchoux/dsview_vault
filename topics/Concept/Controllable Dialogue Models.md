Controllable dialogue models are AI frameworks designed to generate conversational responses that align with specified goals or intents. These models are crucial in applications where dialogue needs to be tailored based on user interactions or game strategies. They enable AI systems to engage in coherent, context-aware conversations while also adhering to predetermined objectives, making them particularly useful in scenarios like negotiation or strategic planning.
