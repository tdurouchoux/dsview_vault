Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) is a framework that combines traditional retrieval systems with generative models. It enables the effective use of external data sources to enhance the generation of responses or reports, ensuring that the output is both relevant and informed by a wide range of information, while focusing on enhancing text generation models by incorporating contextually relevant information retrieved from large data sources, crucial for producing coherent and relevant outputs in AI-driven applications.
