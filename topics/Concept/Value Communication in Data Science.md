Value Communication in Data Science focuses on the methods and practices for articulating the benefits and contributions of data efforts to stakeholders and other business units. It highlights the necessity of ensuring that the impact of data initiatives is clearly communicated, often relying on feedback, testimonials, and advocacy from those who utilize data services. This concept stresses that the perceived value from data work shouldn't be framed solely through quantitative analysis but rather through the narratives shaped by those who benefit from the insights provided.
