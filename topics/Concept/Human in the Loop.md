Human-in-the-Loop is a process or workflow where human judgment and expertise are integrated into the evaluation, refinement, and improvement of AI systems. This involves domain experts labeling data, validating model outputs, providing feedback, and collaboratively refining models and prompts. In the context of AI applications, this approach ensures alignment with real-world requirements, identifies nuances missed by automated metrics, and facilitates iterative improvements through tools and platforms that enable annotation, feedback, and fine-tuning.
