Human-in-the-Loop is a workflow where human reviewers actively participate in the evaluation and improvement of AI systems. In the context of LLM applications, this involves collaborative reviews of traces, creation of golden datasets, and iterative refinement of models and prompts. Tools like Langfuse facilitate this process by enabling teams to annotate data, provide feedback, and guide the training or fine-tuning of AI models, ensuring alignment with human expectations and requirements.
