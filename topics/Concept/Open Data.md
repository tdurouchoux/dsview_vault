Open Data refers to the concept of making data freely available for anyone to use, share, and modify without restrictions. This practice promotes transparency, innovation, and collaboration across various sectors by allowing individuals, organizations, and governments to access valuable data sets. Open Data can contribute to advancements in research, technology, civic engagement, and economic development by enabling the rediscovery of data for new purposes.
