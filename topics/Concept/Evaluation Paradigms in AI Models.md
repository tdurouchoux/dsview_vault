Evaluation paradigms in AI models refer to the methodologies and strategies employed to assess the performance and capabilities of AI systems. The text critiques current evaluation practices that often rely heavily on final answer accuracy without considering the reasoning processes behind those answers. Effective evaluation paradigms would incorporate testing the quality of reasoning traces, the model's ability to handle complexity, and the effectiveness of intermediate solutions, moving beyond simplistic correctness metrics.
