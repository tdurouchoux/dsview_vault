The framework, processes, and policies for governing, securing, and managing data assets within an organization. This includes establishing guidelines for data privacy, security, usability, integrity, and compliance. Effective governance ensures data quality, trustworthiness, and adherence to regulations, which are critical for AI, analytics, and organizational initiatives. It may also involve enforcing policies programmatically, assigning roles, configuring data access, and automating the protection of sensitive data.
