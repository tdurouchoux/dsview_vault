Cross-modal reasoning refers to the ability of a model to draw inferences and make decisions that connect information from multiple sensory modalities (such as visual and textual inputs). This capability is crucial for multimodal models like Gemini, as it allows them to understand and interact with diverse types of data, leading to enhanced comprehension and more meaningful results in applications like education and content summarization.
