MCP Prompts are reusable templates that guide the behavior of large language models (LLMs) in specific workflows. They define structured input formats or instructions for LLMs, ensuring consistent and predictable outputs. Prompts can include parameters to customize the behavior of the LLM, making them useful for complex tasks that require standardized formatting or multi-step interactions. MCP Prompts are exposed as part of the Model Control Protocol, allowing LLMs to leverage them dynamically.
