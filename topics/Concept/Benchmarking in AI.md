The practice of measuring and evaluating the performance of AI models and systems against standard datasets and tasks to gauge their effectiveness, reliability, and efficiency. This process plays a crucial role in guiding research and development in machine learning by influencing funding and direction, while also tracking advancements and setting performance standards across various models, tools, and platforms. Benchmarking must maintain transparency and fairness to avoid biases that can arise from unequal access to data and evaluation conditions.
