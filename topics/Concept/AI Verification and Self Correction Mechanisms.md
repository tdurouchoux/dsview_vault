A systematic approach to ensuring the quality, correctness, and reliability of AI-generated outputs through verification and feedback loops. This includes implementing verification steps (e.g., deterministic checks, tests, evals, and LLM reviews) and embedding self-verification and reflexive mechanisms in AI agents. These mechanisms enable AI models to evaluate, refine, and improve their own work through processes like self-assessment, real-time self-correction, parametric adaptation, and validator-driven feedback. The goal is to catch errors early, reduce manual oversight, and enhance adaptability, particularly in long-horizon tasks and high-stakes domains.
