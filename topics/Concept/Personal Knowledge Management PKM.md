Personal Knowledge Management (PKM) refers to the processes and tools used by individuals to collect, organize, store, and retrieve knowledge. It emphasizes the importance of structuring information in a way that enhances understanding, retention, and application. PKM systems often leverage technologies like wikis, note-taking apps, and AI tools to automate and streamline knowledge organization, making it easier for individuals to manage and utilize their accumulated information over time.
