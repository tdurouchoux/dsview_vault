Delegated workflows refer to an interaction paradigm where knowledge workers instruct AI systems, such as large language models (LLMs), to perform tasks on their behalf over extended sessions. This concept emphasizes the delegation of complex, multi-step document editing tasks to AI, requiring the system to understand and execute instructions accurately while maintaining the integrity of the original document. Delegated workflows are particularly relevant in professional settings where repetitive or intricate document manipulations are required, and they serve as a testbed for evaluating the reliability and robustness of AI systems in real-world applications.
