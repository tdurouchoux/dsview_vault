Post-training reasoning involves optimizing an agent's behavior through reinforcement learning (RL) and supervised fine-tuning to internalize successful reasoning patterns and tool-use strategies. Unlike in-context reasoning, this paradigm consolidates capabilities into the model's weights, enabling durable improvements that generalize across tasks. Post-training reasoning is essential for aligning agentic systems with long-horizon rewards, such as correctness, safety, and efficiency, and is often used to train models like DeepSeek-R1 and Search-R1.
