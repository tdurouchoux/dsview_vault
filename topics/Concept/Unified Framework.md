A unified framework in the context of data science and machine learning refers to an organized and comprehensive structure that categorizes and integrates various methodologies and approaches. It helps streamline the analysis of different techniques, ensuring coherence and clarity in the evaluation of their performance across specific applications or domains. This is particularly useful for developing best practices and guiding future research by clearly delineating how various methods relate to one another.
