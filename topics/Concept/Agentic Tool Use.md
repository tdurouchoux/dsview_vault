Agentic Tool Use, also referred to as Tool-Augmented Reasoning, involves the intelligent integration of external modules, APIs, or computational resources to enhance an agent's capabilities. This includes reasoning about when and how to use tools, selecting the appropriate tool, and executing operations to overcome limitations such as outdated knowledge, imprecise calculations, or lack of access to private information. It enables agents to extend their reasoning, improve precision, and dynamically adapt across domains by integrating external tools into their workflows, facilitating real-time decision-making, data analysis, and automation.
