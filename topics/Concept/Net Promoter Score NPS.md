Net Promoter Score (NPS) is a metric used to gauge customer satisfaction and loyalty by asking respondents how likely they are to recommend a product or service to others. This concept serves as a simplistic yet effective measure of perceived value for a data team's output. It provides insights into how stakeholders view the efficacy of data initiatives and can inform a broader understanding of the data team's ROI. A higher NPS score suggests that the data team is successfully delivering valuable insights and tools to support decision-making across the organization.
