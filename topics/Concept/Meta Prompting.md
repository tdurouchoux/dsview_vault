Meta-prompting is a specific technique within prompt optimization that utilizes one language model (LLM) to analyze and refine prompts for another LLM. It involves running the target LLM over a set of examples, scoring outputs, and generating improved prompts based on evaluation of current prompt effectiveness. This approach allows for iterative improvement and can uncover complex conditional behaviors that enhance the model's ability to follow nuanced instructions.
