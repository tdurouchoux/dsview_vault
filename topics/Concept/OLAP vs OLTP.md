OLAP (Online Analytical Processing) and OLTP (Online Transaction Processing) are two types of database processing systems designed for different tasks. OLAP is optimized for querying and analyzing large volumes of data, allowing users to perform complex calculations and aggregations. OLTP, in contrast, is designed for managing transactional data and ensuring data integrity during insert, update, and delete operations, focusing on performance in handling many short online transactions.
