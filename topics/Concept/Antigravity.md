Antigravity is a hypothetical concept of creating a gravitational force that acts in opposition to the force of gravity. In theoretical physics and science fiction, antigravity is often discussed as a means to counter Earth's gravitational pull, enabling levitation or propulsion without traditional mechanical means. While antigravity remains unproven and is not currently achievable with known physics, it is a popular topic in speculative research and futuristic technologies.
