Steerable AI systems refer to artificial intelligence architectures that allow users to guide and adjust the output of the model dynamically. In the context of Copilot Workspace, users can modify the specifications and plans generated by the AI to ensure that the suggestions and code produced meet their requirements. This concept emphasizes user control and adaptability, making the AI's output more relevant and correct in software development tasks.
