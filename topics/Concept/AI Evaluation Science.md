AI Evaluation Science is an interdisciplinary field focused on developing rigorous, systematic methods to assess the performance, safety, robustness, alignment, and risks of advanced AI systems. This includes creating metrics, benchmarks, and testing protocols (such as development evaluations, assurance evaluations, external evaluations, and red teaming) to evaluate AI models and systems in real-world conditions, ensuring they meet regulatory and societal expectations.
