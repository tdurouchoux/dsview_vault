The Dashboard Development Cycle is the iterative process of designing, developing, and refining dashboards to ensure they meet user needs and organizational goals. This cycle includes steps such as defining the audience and objectives, selecting relevant data, prototyping, reviewing for clarity and actionability, and iterating based on feedback. The cycle emphasizes the importance of collaboration between data practitioners and domain experts to ensure the final product is both technically sound and practically useful.
