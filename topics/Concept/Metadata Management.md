Metadata management refers to the process of collecting, storing, organizing, and maintaining metadata—data that describes other data. It is essential for understanding data lineage, ensuring data quality, enabling discovery, and supporting governance. Effective metadata management helps organizations track the origin, usage, and relationships of data assets, facilitating compliance, collaboration, and decision-making. Tools like DataHub centralize metadata to provide a unified view of an organization's data ecosystem.
