Product-Market Fit is a stage in a startup's development where a product has successfully met the demands of the market, leading to substantial customer satisfaction and growth. Understanding PMF is crucial for startups as it guides product development and business strategy, ensuring that offerings align closely with user needs. The notion often influences how features are prioritized in software development, especially in traditional SaaS environments.
