A detailed, sequenced plan that outlines the steps, ownership, timelines, and dependencies required to implement a data strategy. It prioritizes initiatives based on business value and effort, ensuring alignment between technical feasibility and organizational goals. A well-constructed roadmap includes foundational initiatives (e.g., data quality improvement) and value-generating use cases (e.g., AI tools, dashboards), with clear accountability and executive sponsorship.
