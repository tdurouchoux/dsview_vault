Causal inference is a field in statistics and data science that focuses on identifying and estimating causal relationships between variables. It aims to determine how a change in one variable causally affects another variable, often using techniques such as randomized controlled trials, observational studies, and statistical models. This field is critical for understanding the effects of interventions and for making informed decisions based on data.
