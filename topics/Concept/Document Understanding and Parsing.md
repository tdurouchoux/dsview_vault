Document understanding and parsing refers to the ability of machine learning models to process and comprehend different elements of documents such as texts, images, tables, and layout features. This includes interpreting complex layouts and structures, extracting and categorizing structured information from various document types, which can include PDFs, scientific papers, and multimodal documents. The goal is to convert unstructured or semi-structured information into structured data formats for applications, data analysis tools, or machine learning models.
