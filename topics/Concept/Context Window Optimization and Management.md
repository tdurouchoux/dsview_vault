The practice of designing, curating, and optimizing the information provided to AI models within their context window. This includes managing the amount of information loaded into the context window, organizing context into structured formats (e.g., persistent files, knowledge graphs), and dynamically adjusting the context to ensure relevance and efficiency. Effective context optimization and management ensures that AI systems focus on the most pertinent details while avoiding unnecessary or excessive information that could degrade performance.
