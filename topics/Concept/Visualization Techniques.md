Visualization techniques encompass a variety of methods to effectively represent data visually, allowing for better interpretation and understanding. This includes techniques for different data types and structures, such as dynamic graphs, text visualization, sentiment analysis, bar graphs, line charts, heat maps, and more unconventional methods like infographics, which can be tailored for specific use cases in scientific research and data analysis.
