Tool selection for AI agents refers to the process of choosing the appropriate set of external tools that augment the capabilities of an AI agent. The selection is crucial as it directly impacts the agent's performance in accomplishing tasks. Tool inventories can consist of various functionalities, including data analysis, information retrieval, and action execution. Selecting the right tools involves understanding the agent's environment, the tasks it needs to perform, and the strengths and weaknesses of available tools.
