Mixture of Experts (MoE) is a model architecture concept that involves training multiple models (experts) and routing inputs to select the most relevant experts for a given task. This architecture allows for increased model capacity and efficiency by leveraging specialized sub-models for different types of data or tasks while enhancing model efficiency through conditional computation, activating a subset of 'experts' (sub-models) for each input.
