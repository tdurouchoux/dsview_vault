PivotTables are a data processing feature in spreadsheet applications that allow users to summarize and analyze data interactively. They enable users to reorganize and aggregate data for reporting purposes, allowing for quick visualization of patterns and insights without altering the original dataset. PivotTables can group data along various dimensions, providing flexibility for data querying and exploration in a concise format.
