The AI Continent Action Plan is a strategic initiative by the European Commission aimed at positioning the EU as a global leader in artificial intelligence (AI). It focuses on fostering innovation, investment, and infrastructure development to ensure Europe's competitiveness in AI and cloud technologies. The plan includes measures to accelerate the adoption of AI in critical sectors, support research and development, and enhance digital sovereignty through strategic initiatives like the Cloud and AI Development Act (CADA).
