Multi-Token Prediction (MTP) is a training technique and objective used in language models to improve prediction accuracy, efficiency, and performance. Unlike traditional single-token prediction, MTP predicts multiple tokens simultaneously, enabling the model to capture richer contextual information and dependencies between tokens. This approach enhances training efficiency, generalization, and the ability to generate coherent and contextually relevant text. MTP is particularly useful in tasks requiring precise sequence generation, such as OCR and document understanding, and can also accelerate inference through techniques like speculative decoding.
