Hackable AI Systems are platforms or tools designed to be customizable, modifiable, and extensible by users. These systems provide open-source codebases, APIs, or plugin architectures that allow developers and enthusiasts to tailor functionality to their specific needs. Hackable AI systems empower users to innovate, debug, and enhance the tool independently, fostering a community-driven approach to development. OpenClaw exemplifies this concept by enabling users to build their own extensions, modify behaviors, or even self-host the system.
