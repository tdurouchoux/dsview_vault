Semantic relations describe the connections and meanings associated with entities within knowledge graphs. These relationships often include hierarchical linkages, such as 'is a' and 'part of,' and associative linkages, such as 'related to' or 'affects.' Understanding and interpreting these relations is vital for effective data integration, reasoning, and retrieval tasks in both KGs and NLP.
