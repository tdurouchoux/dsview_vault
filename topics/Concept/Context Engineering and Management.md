The practice of designing, curating, and optimizing the information provided to AI models and agents during interactions. This includes organizing context into structured formats (e.g., persistent files, knowledge graphs), dynamically managing the context window, and integrating both structured and unstructured data to enable context-aware AI applications. Effective context engineering and management ensures that AI systems have access to relevant details while avoiding unnecessary or excessive information that could degrade performance.
