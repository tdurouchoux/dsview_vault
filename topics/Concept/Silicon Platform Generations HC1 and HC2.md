Taalas' silicon platforms are categorized into generations (e.g., HC1 and HC2) to denote advancements in hardware design. HC1 is the first-generation platform, optimized for mid-sized models and achieving significant speed and efficiency gains. HC2, the second-generation platform, offers higher density, faster execution, and improved performance, enabling the deployment of frontier LLMs with even greater efficiency.
