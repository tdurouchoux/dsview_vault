The coefficient of correlation is a statistical measure that represents the strength and direction of a relationship between two variables. It ranges from -1 to 1, where 1 indicates a perfect positive correlation, -1 indicates a perfect negative correlation, and 0 indicates no correlation. This concept is fundamental in data analysis as it helps to determine relationships between variables, guiding data scientists in predictive modeling and regression analysis.
