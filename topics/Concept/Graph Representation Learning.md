Graph representation learning focuses on the task of learning low-dimensional embeddings for nodes, edges, or entire graphs while preserving their structural information, including concepts for learning vector representations of nodes so that similar nodes are close together in the vector space. These representations are useful in various applications such as social network analysis, molecular property prediction, and more, enabling the use of traditional machine learning algorithms on graph data.
