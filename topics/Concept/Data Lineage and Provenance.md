Data Lineage and Provenance refer to the processes and technologies used to track the origin, history, and transformations of data as it flows from its source to its destination. This includes tracing data through various systems, transformations, and processes, such as SQL queries (e.g., CTEs, joins, filters, and aggregations), to ensure transparency, accountability, and traceability. These concepts are critical for data governance, compliance, debugging, and maintaining data quality and integrity in complex workflows and AI systems.
