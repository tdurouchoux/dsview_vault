Subgraph sampling is a pivotal technique used in training Graph Neural Networks, where small subsets (subgraphs) of a larger graph are sampled for training purposes. This allows the model to learn from manageable chunks of data while still capturing the overall structure and relationships within the full graph. Methods for subgraph sampling can be dynamic or batch-based, impacting the efficiency and scalability of GNN training.
