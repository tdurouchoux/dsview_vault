Dashboard Design Fundamentals refer to the core principles and best practices for creating effective, actionable, and trustworthy dashboards. These include understanding the audience, defining clear objectives, ensuring data quality, maintaining visual restraint, and prioritizing action-oriented insights over mere data presentation. Key contributors like Edward Tufte, Stephen Few, and Andy Kirk emphasize that graphical excellence is achieved when complex ideas are communicated with clarity, precision, and efficiency. Effective dashboards are not just visually polished but also align with the decision-making needs of their users.
