Pseudocode representation is a method of describing algorithms or computational processes using a high-level, informal syntax that is easy to read and understand. It abstracts the specifics of programming language syntax, focusing instead on the logic and structure of the code. This technique is often used to design algorithms before they are implemented in actual code, making it easier for developers to conceptualize and communicate their ideas.
