LLM-as-a-Judge is an evaluation technique where a language model (LLM) is used to assess the outputs of another LLM or AI system. This approach leverages the judgment capabilities of advanced LLMs to provide automated evaluations of performance, cost, latency, quality, and accuracy. It is particularly useful for scalable and cost-effective evaluation of AI systems, enabling continuous monitoring and improvement without heavy reliance on manual human reviews. This technique can be applied to various tasks, including text-to-SQL output evaluation.
