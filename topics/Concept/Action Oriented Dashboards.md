Action-Oriented Dashboards are designed to support specific decisions by focusing on a single, well-defined question or objective. These dashboards prioritize clarity and relevance, ensuring that every element on the page serves a clear purpose. They often follow principles like 'starting with the so-what,' where the most critical insight is highlighted first, and the dashboard is structured to guide the user toward a decision. This approach contrasts with dashboards that merely present data without context or actionable insights.
