Evolutionary optimization refers to algorithms that explore the space of possible prompts through mechanisms similar to biological evolution, including mutation and selection. This approach involves generating multiple candidate prompts, evaluating their performance, and iteratively refining them based on their effectiveness. Evolutionary optimization aims to overcome local optima by fostering diversity in the prompt set, thus enhancing the overall robustness of the language model's outputs.
