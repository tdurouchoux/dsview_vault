Red Teaming in AI refers to the practice of proactively testing and evaluating AI systems for vulnerabilities and weaknesses. This involves simulating real-world attacks or testing AI systems with external feedback to identify potential risks and enhance the system's resilience before public deployment. It helps identify weaknesses and improve safety measures, particularly as generative models are integrated into various products and services.
