Visual Analytics Governance encompasses the policies, processes, and standards that ensure dashboards and visual analytics tools are developed, deployed, and maintained in a way that aligns with organizational goals and user needs. It involves defining ownership, setting design principles, ensuring data quality, and establishing guidelines for tool usage. Effective governance prevents the proliferation of low-quality or irrelevant dashboards and ensures that analytics artifacts are trustworthy and actionable.
