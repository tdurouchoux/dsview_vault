Data Governance and Policies refer to the frameworks, rules, and processes that manage data availability, usability, integrity, security, and compliance within an organization. This includes establishing guidelines for data usage, access, and protection to ensure accuracy, consistency, and reliability. It also involves enforcing policies programmatically, assigning roles to users, configuring data access, automating the masking of sensitive data, and maintaining compliance with regulations and ethical standards. Tools like Open Policy Agent (OPA) may be used to enforce and manage these policies.
