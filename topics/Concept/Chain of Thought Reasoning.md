Chain-of-Thought reasoning refers to a cognitive process employed by AI models where they articulate their intermediate reasoning steps leading to a final answer. This method improves the interpretability and effectiveness of AI models, particularly in tasks that require complex decision-making or problem-solving, by making their thought processes more transparent and understandable. It serves both as a tool for enhancing problem-solving capabilities and as a mechanism for monitoring and verifying AI alignment with human intent.
