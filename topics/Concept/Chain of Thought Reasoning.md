A prompting technique wherein models are guided to explain their reasoning through a structured thought process, enhancing their ability to make informed decisions. Chain-of-Thought reasoning refers to a cognitive process employed by AI models where they articulate their intermediate reasoning steps leading to a final answer. This method improves the interpretability and effectiveness of AI models, particularly in tasks that require complex decision-making or problem-solving, by making their thought processes more transparent and understandable.
