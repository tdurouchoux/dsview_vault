Medical document understanding involves extracting structured data from unstructured medical documents, such as lab reports, clinical notes, and other textual information. This process is crucial for transforming unstructured data into a format that can be easily analyzed and utilized for medical decision-making. MedGemma 1.5 4B improves accuracy in extracting structured data from medical lab reports, enhancing its utility in medical document understanding.
