Jailbreaking in the context of AI refers to techniques used to bypass or circumvent the safeguards and restrictions implemented in AI models to prevent misuse. These techniques can range from narrow, non-universal methods that exploit specific vulnerabilities to broader, universal methods that unblock a wide range of capabilities. Jailbreaking is a critical concern for AI safety and security, as it can enable harmful or unintended uses of AI systems.
