A defense-in-depth strategy is a layered approach to security and risk mitigation that combines multiple defensive mechanisms to protect a system. In the context of AI models like Fable 5, this strategy involves implementing strong safeguards, monitoring for potential breaches, and retaining customer data to quickly detect and mitigate jailbreaks or other attacks. The goal is to make it difficult for attackers to succeed and to minimize the impact of any successful breaches.
