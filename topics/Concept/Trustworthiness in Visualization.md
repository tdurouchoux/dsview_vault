Trustworthiness in Visualization refers to the degree of confidence users can place in a dashboard or visual analytics artifact. It is built on foundational layers such as clarity, accuracy, transparency, and relevance. Trustworthiness is compromised when dashboards contain unexplained data, inconsistencies, or visual elements that mislead or confuse users. Andy Kirk and other experts emphasize that trust is a prerequisite for effective data communication and decision-making.
