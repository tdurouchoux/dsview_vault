AI safety and safeguards refer to the mechanisms, protocols, and guidelines implemented to prevent the misuse of AI systems and ensure their ethical and responsible operation. These include technical safeguards such as content filters, usage policies, and restrictions to mitigate risks like generating harmful, illegal, or unethical content (e.g., hate speech, harassment, or dangerous content). Safety policies also guide the development and evaluation of AI models, prioritizing neutrality, factuality, and alignment with global user needs. While these measures aim to be robust, achieving perfect resistance against misuse remains a challenge.
