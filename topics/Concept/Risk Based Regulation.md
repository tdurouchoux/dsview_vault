Risk-based regulation is an approach to governance that evaluates and manages risks associated with different activities or technologies, tailoring regulatory requirements based on the potential level of harm they might cause. In the context of artificial intelligence, this means that AI systems are classified by risk level, with stricter regulations applied to those deemed high-risk. This is intended to ensure that innovations can proceed without undue burdens while maintaining public safety.
