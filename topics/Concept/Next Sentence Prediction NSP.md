Next Sentence Prediction (NSP) is a task used in pre-training language models like BERT, where the model learns to predict if one sentence logically follows another. This task enhances the model's ability to understand relationships between sentences, which is crucial for tasks like natural language inference and question answering. NSP is performed by providing pairs of sentences during training, with the model learning to differentiate between actual consecutive sentences and randomly selected sentences.
