A theoretical framework used to understand the probability of failure as a function of time, particularly in the context of AI agents performing tasks. This model predicts that the chance of failure remains constant regardless of how long a task has been running, leading to exponentially declining success rates over time. It is akin to models used in survival analysis and can be used to predict the performance of AI agents over varying durations of tasks.
