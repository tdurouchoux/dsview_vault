Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) is a critical preliminary step in data analysis that involves examining raw data, traces, and model outputs to identify patterns, anomalies, trends, and areas for improvement. It employs summary statistics, visual methods, and in-depth analyses such as train vs. test distribution checks and temporal pattern analysis of target variables. EDA is essential for understanding the behavior of AI models, ensuring meaningful evaluation metrics, and guiding robust model development for real-world applications.
