Agentic search, also referred to as Agentic RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation), is a dynamic and iterative process where autonomous agents retrieve, synthesize, and integrate information to support reasoning and decision-making. Unlike traditional RAG, agentic search emphasizes adaptive query formulation, structured retrieval, and reflective integration of evidence, often leveraging tools like search APIs, knowledge graphs, and structured memory. This approach enhances the accuracy, context-awareness, and relevance of retrieved information, particularly for tasks requiring multi-hop reasoning, cross-source corroboration, and real-time adaptation to evolving contexts.
