Human-AI cooperation focuses on the synergetic interaction between human users and AI systems to achieve shared goals. This concept encompasses the design and implementation of AI that can understand and anticipate human needs, facilitating better collaboration. It plays a significant role in applications where AI systems need to negotiate, persuade, or adapt to human operators, such as in gaming, customer service, and interactive systems.
