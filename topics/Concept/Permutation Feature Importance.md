Permutation feature importance is a method for estimating the importance of a feature in a predictive model by measuring the change in the model's performance metric when the values of that feature are permuted (shuffled). This technique helps to quantify the impact of specific features on the model and aids in model interpretation, as it directly correlates a feature's importance with the model's predictive accuracy.
