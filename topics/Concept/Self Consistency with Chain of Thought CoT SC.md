Self-Consistency with Chain-of-Thought (CoT-SC) is an enhancement to the Chain-of-Thought prompting strategy, where multiple independent reasoning paths (chains of thought) are generated for a given task. After evaluating these paths, the one with the highest score or best outcome is chosen as the final answer. This approach improves the resolution of complex tasks by ensuring a more reliable response from the language model through diversity in the reasoning pathways explored.
