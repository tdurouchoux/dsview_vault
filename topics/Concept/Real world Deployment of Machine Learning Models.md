The real-world deployment of machine learning models refers to the practical implementation and integration of trained models into production systems where they are used in actual business operations. This involves addressing various challenges, such as scalability, maintenance, data privacy, and ensuring the models generalize well to new, unseen data. In the context of financial fraud detection, effective deployment is critical for timely identification and action against fraudulent transactions.
