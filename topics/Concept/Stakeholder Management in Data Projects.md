The practice of identifying, engaging, and communicating with stakeholders throughout a data project lifecycle. It involves understanding their needs, aligning expectations, and ensuring their involvement in key decisions. Effective stakeholder management ensures that the delivered solution addresses real business problems and is adopted post-delivery. This includes recognizing decision-makers, champions, and informed parties, tailoring communication to their roles, and securing necessary resources and support. It is crucial for the success of data science initiatives, as it helps in defining clear business problems, securing sponsorship, and ensuring the adoption of project outcomes.
