Human-AI collaboration refers to the synergistic partnership between human users and artificial intelligence systems, where both parties contribute their unique strengths to achieve a shared goal. This concept encompasses the design and implementation of AI that can understand and anticipate human needs, facilitating better collaboration and enhancing decision-making in various applications. Effective human-AI collaboration involves user-centered systems that adapt to human inputs and intentions.
