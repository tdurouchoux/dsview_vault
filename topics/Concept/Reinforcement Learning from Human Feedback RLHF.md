Reinforcement Learning from Human Feedback (RLHF) is a machine learning paradigm that incorporates human judgments to enhance the training of models. By collecting feedback from users on model outputs, this approach optimizes performance and aligns machine learning systems more closely with human values and preferences. This method is especially valuable in complex decision-making tasks, where traditional reward functions may be insufficient, enabling a fine-tuned model based on expressed human preferences.
