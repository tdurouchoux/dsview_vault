Persistent Memory, also referred to as Auto Memory, is an AI feature that automatically captures, stores, and retains insights, patterns, preferences, and user-specific context learned during interactions. It enables AI systems to recall historical interactions over extended periods, improving performance, adaptability, and personalization in future sessions. This capability is essential for building long-term, trustworthy AI assistants that maintain continuity in conversations and adapt dynamically to user needs.
