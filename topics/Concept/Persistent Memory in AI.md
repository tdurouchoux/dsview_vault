A structured system for persisting and retrieving information across AI sessions, also referred to as Auto Memory. It captures, stores, and retains insights, patterns, preferences, and user-specific context learned during interactions, ensuring continuity and reducing redundant explanations. This capability enables AI systems to recall historical interactions over extended periods, improving performance, adaptability, and personalization in future sessions, and is essential for building long-term, trustworthy AI assistants.
