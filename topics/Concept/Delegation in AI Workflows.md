The practice of assigning tasks to AI models, particularly in complex or multi-step workflows, while ensuring clear success criteria, constraints, and verification mechanisms. Delegation shifts the focus from micro-management to high-level planning and review, enabling parallel execution of tasks and scaling of AI-assisted workflows. This concept is particularly relevant in professional settings where repetitive or intricate document manipulations are required, serving as a testbed for evaluating the reliability and robustness of AI systems in real-world applications.
