A method of converting text into numerical representations, known as embeddings, which represent semantic meaning in a vector space. Text embeddings are dense vector representations generated by various algorithms capturing the semantic meaning of words and phrases. These embeddings enable applications in natural language processing tasks, such as searching for relevant documents and enhancing generative models through context retrieval, while preserving contextual relationships and similarities between different texts.
