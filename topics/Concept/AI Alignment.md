AI Alignment and LLM Alignment are fields of research focused on ensuring that artificial intelligence systems, particularly powerful AI and Large Language Models, act in ways that are aligned with human values and objectives. This encompasses strategies to make AI's goals compatible with human intentions, including fine-tuning models to prevent adverse outcomes, avoid generating harmful or biased content, and enhance user trust, thereby harnessing the full potential of AI technologies in a responsible manner.
