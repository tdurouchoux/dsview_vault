DAG visualization provides a graphical representation of the structure and flow of a directed acyclic graph that defines a workflow. It enables users to see task dependencies, execution paths, and the overall layout of the pipeline, aiding in better understanding and management of complex workflows. Visualization tools are crucial for debugging and optimizing workflows by highlighting potential bottlenecks and flow anomalies.
