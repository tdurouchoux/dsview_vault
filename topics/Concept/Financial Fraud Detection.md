Financial fraud detection encompasses the methodologies and technologies used to identify and prevent fraudulent activities within financial transactions. This field often employs advanced machine learning and data analysis techniques to analyze transaction patterns, customer behaviors, and anomalies. The increasing complexity of transactions, driven by globalization and technological advancement, necessitates sophisticated tools and algorithms, such as Graph Neural Networks, to enhance detection accuracy and efficiency.
