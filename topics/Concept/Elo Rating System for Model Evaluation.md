The Elo rating system, originally used in chess, is adapted for evaluating and ranking models or AI-generated outputs. It assigns numerical ratings to models or hypotheses based on head-to-head comparisons, tournaments, or self-play debates. This method provides a transparent and intuitive way to assess relative performance, with higher Elo ratings indicating stronger or higher-quality results. It is widely used in platforms like TabArena and AI systems for auto-evaluation purposes.
