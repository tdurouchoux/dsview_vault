The Cloud and AI Sovereignty Framework is a structured approach introduced by the Cloud and AI Development Act (CADA) to assess and ensure the sovereignty of cloud and AI services used by public sector bodies. It defines four assurance levels based on criteria such as data storage location, independence from third countries, EU ownership and control, and transparency of the software supply chain. This framework helps public administrations mitigate risks associated with strategic dependencies on non-EU providers.
