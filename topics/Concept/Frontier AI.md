Frontier AI, also referred to as Powerful AI, represents advanced AI technologies that push the boundaries of current capabilities. These systems are characterized by cutting-edge, high-performance models or novel applications in areas like industrial automation, physical AI, biology, programming, mathematics, engineering, and writing. Frontier AI is envisioned to possess human-like or superhuman intelligence, enabling it to perform complex tasks across various domains through multiple interfaces such as text, audio, video, and internet access. The development of these AI systems is expected to have profound implications for scientific progress, economic development, and societal transformation, while also enhancing competitiveness and leadership in AI innovation.
