Open source collaboration is a development model that encourages sharing, modification, and enhancement of software among a community of contributors. This model fosters transparency and collective improvement, allowing for faster development cycles and adoption. As demonstrated by Splink, open source collaboration can break down organizational silos and enable collaboration across different sectors, leading to stronger, community-driven software solutions that benefit a wide range of users.
