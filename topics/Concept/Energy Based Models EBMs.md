Energy-Based Models (EBMs) are a class of machine learning models that assign a scalar energy value to each possible configuration of the input data. The goal of EBMs is to learn a model that assigns low energy to compatible or realistic configurations and high energy to incompatible or unrealistic ones. EBMs provide a unifying framework for understanding various self-supervised learning approaches, including joint-embedding architectures and generative models.
