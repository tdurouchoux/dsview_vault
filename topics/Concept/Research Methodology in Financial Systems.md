Research methodology in financial systems involves the systematic approaches and techniques used to investigate and analyze financial phenomena. This includes the design of studies, data collection strategies, and analytical methods that researchers apply to assess performance, detect anomalies or fraud, and validate models within the context of finance. Integrating modern techniques, such as GNNs, into this methodology can enhance the capabilities and effectiveness of fraud detection measures.
