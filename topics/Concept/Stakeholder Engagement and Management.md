Stakeholder Engagement and Management in Data Projects pertains to actively involving all parties that have a stake in the outcomes of data initiatives. It encompasses understanding their needs, managing expectations, and ensuring that data outputs align with their requirements for decision-making. This includes identifying, analyzing, and engaging stakeholders, establishing expectations, gathering input, and ensuring support throughout the project lifecycle to enhance the impact of data analysis and deliver value aligned with business objectives.
