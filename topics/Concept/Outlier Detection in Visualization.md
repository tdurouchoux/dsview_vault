Outlier detection in visualization refers to the process of identifying anomalous data points that significantly differ from the majority of data. Handling outliers is crucial in creating effective visualizations, as they can misleadingly skew patterns and interpretations. Best practices include checking for outliers before plotting data in formats like heatmaps, which rely on color gradients to convey information.
