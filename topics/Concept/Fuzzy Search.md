Fuzzy Search refers to the ability to find items that are approximately or partially matching a query, rather than requiring an exact match. This is particularly useful in applications like spell-checking, record linkage, or search engines where minor variations in data (e.g., typos, synonyms) should still yield relevant results. USearch supports fuzzy search through its approximate nearest neighbor search capabilities and user-defined metrics, enabling flexible and tolerant matching.
