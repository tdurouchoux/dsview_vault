Preference alignment involves tuning language models to better align their responses with human preferences and expectations. This process incorporates methods to reduce undesirable outputs, such as toxicity or hallucinations, ensuring that the generated text is not only useful but also safe and appropriate for users. Techniques like Direct Preference Optimization (DPO) and Proximal Policy Optimization (PPO) are utilized to enhance how models generate responses based on human feedback.
