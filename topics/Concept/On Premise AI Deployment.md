On-Premise AI Deployment refers to the practice of hosting AI systems locally on a user's own hardware (e.g., personal computers, Raspberry Pi) rather than relying on cloud-based services. This approach prioritizes data privacy, control, and customization, as all computations and data storage occur within the user's infrastructure. On-premise AI is particularly appealing for organizations or individuals concerned about data security, compliance, or latency. OpenClaw supports on-premise deployment, aligning with its ethos of user autonomy.
