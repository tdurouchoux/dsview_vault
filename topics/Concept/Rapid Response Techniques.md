Rapid response techniques are adaptive strategies designed to identify and mitigate neurological attacks or misuse of machine learning models, particularly in the context of large language models (LLMs). Unlike static defenses that aim for comprehensive protection against all possible attacks, rapid response emphasizes quick detection and adaptation to emerging threats, enabling systems to proactively block harmful behaviors after observing a limited number of malicious attempts.
