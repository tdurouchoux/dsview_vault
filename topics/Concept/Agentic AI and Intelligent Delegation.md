Agentic AI and Intelligent Delegation represent a paradigm shift from isolated AI agents to orchestrated, multi-agent ecosystems capable of dynamically decomposing and executing complex goals. These systems emphasize adaptive frameworks for task allocation, delegation, and collaboration among AI agents or humans. Key characteristics include goal decomposition, inter-agent communication, verifiable task execution, scalable coordination, and systemic resilience. 

In such systems, multiple specialized agents collaborate to achieve high-level objectives through collaborative reasoning, multi-step planning, and persistent memory. The framework ensures clear role definitions, intent clarity, and trust mechanisms while supporting distributed and adaptive behaviors. This approach mimics human-like reasoning and collaboration, leveraging automated feedback and iterative processes to improve performance, accuracy, and adaptability in dynamic environments.
