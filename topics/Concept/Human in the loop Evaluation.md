Human-in-the-loop evaluation refers to a methodology where human judgement is integrated into the assessment process of generated content, particularly in summarization tasks. This approach acknowledges the necessity for human evaluators to provide insight into nuances, context, and quality that automated metrics may overlook. While human evaluation can yield more accurate quality assessments, it often comes with scalability and cost implications, pushing for a balance with automated metrics.
