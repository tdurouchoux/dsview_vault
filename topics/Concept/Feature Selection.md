Feature selection is the process of identifying and selecting a subset of relevant features for use in model construction. The goal is to reduce the dimensionality of the input feature space, improving model performance, decreasing training time, and enhancing model interpretability. Various methods exist for feature selection, including filter methods (which assess features based on their statistical properties), wrapper methods (which consider the combination of features based on their performance with a model), and embedded methods (which perform feature selection as part of the model training process).
