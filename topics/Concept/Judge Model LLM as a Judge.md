A Judge Model, also referred to as LLM-as-a-Judge, is a specialized model or technique used to evaluate or synthesize the outputs of other models or AI systems. In this role, the judge model or LLM reviews responses generated by other models, assesses their quality, coherence, and accuracy, and provides structured analysis or automated evaluations. This approach is particularly valuable in Model Fusion, where it identifies consensus points, contradictions, and blind spots to generate a final, fused response. Additionally, it enables scalable and cost-effective evaluation of AI systems, reducing reliance on manual human reviews while supporting continuous monitoring and improvement across tasks such as text-to-SQL output evaluation.
