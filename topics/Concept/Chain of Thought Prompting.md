Chain-of-thought prompting is a technique where prompts encourage a language model to articulate its reasoning or thought process before delivering a final answer. By guiding the model to explore its reasoning, this approach helps reduce errors, such as hallucinations, and improves overall response quality. It allows models to think step-by-step through a problem and breaks tasks into manageable segments, which is particularly useful for tasks requiring multi-step reasoning.
