Benchmarking in data science refers to the process of evaluating a model or algorithm's performance against a standard or a set of criteria. It helps in comparing different models or approaches to ascertain the most effective one for a given task. Additionally, it encompasses the testing of system or component performance against a standard to evaluate capabilities, often involving predefined tests to measure speed, memory usage, and efficiency in data handling tasks.
