The Next-Token Diffusion Framework is a generative approach used in VibeVoice models that combines the strengths of Large Language Models (LLMs) and diffusion models. The LLM is employed to understand textual context and dialogue flow, while the diffusion head generates high-fidelity acoustic details. This framework ensures coherent and natural-sounding speech synthesis and accurate transcription in ASR tasks.
