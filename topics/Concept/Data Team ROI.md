Data Team ROI (Return on Investment) refers to the value and impact that a data team brings to an organization. It encompasses the outcomes derived from data-related activities, such as data analysis, modeling, and infrastructure improvements. Measuring Data Team ROI involves assessing how data initiatives contribute to business goals, enhance decision-making, and support overall organizational success. This concept is crucial for justifying data team investments and demonstrating their strategic importance within the company.
