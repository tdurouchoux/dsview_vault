The process of controlling, optimizing, and managing the information included in the context window of AI systems to improve performance and efficiency. This involves strategies such as auto-summarization, context compaction (summarizing or offloading older context), dynamic context injection, tool call offloading, skills (progressive disclosure of tools), and leveraging persistent artifacts (e.g., plan.md). The goal is to ensure the AI focuses on the most relevant information while avoiding context overflow, unnecessary distractions, or performance degradation, enabling long, uninterrupted interactions.
