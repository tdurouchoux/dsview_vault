The practice of managing and optimizing the context window of AI tools and models to ensure critical information is preserved and efficiently utilized. This includes techniques such as leveraging persistent artifacts (e.g., plan.md), auto-compaction features, curating and organizing context into structured formats (e.g., persistent files, knowledge graphs), and dynamically adjusting the context to maintain relevance and avoid performance degradation. Effective context window management enables long, uninterrupted development sessions and ensures AI systems focus on the most pertinent details.
