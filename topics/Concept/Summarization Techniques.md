Summarization techniques are methods used in natural language processing to condense text documents into shorter forms, preserving the essential information and meaning. These techniques can be categorized into extractive summarization, where key sentences are selected from the original text, and abstractive summarization, which involves generating new sentences that convey the same information. Evaluating the effectiveness of summarization models often involves comparing the generated summaries to reference summaries using metrics such as ROUGE or BLEU.
