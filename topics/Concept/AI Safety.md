The field focused on ensuring that artificial intelligence systems are beneficial, safe, and do not pose risks to humanity. AI safety involves evaluating the safety, security, and risks of advanced AI systems through international collaboration, technical solutions, ethical guidelines, and regulatory frameworks. It encompasses techniques and policies to prevent AI from causing harm, whether through unintended actions, misuse, or malicious intent, while fostering cooperation, sharing best practices, and conducting joint evaluations to enhance global AI safety and trustworthiness. This includes addressing issues such as bias, privacy, security, and the societal impact of AI, as well as ensuring transparency, fairness, and respect for human rights in AI development and deployment.
