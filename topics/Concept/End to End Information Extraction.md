End-to-end information extraction refers to a comprehensive approach that covers all steps from raw data input to the final structured output, typically in the context of NLP. This methodology ensures that the entire workflow from data processing, through various transformations, to the final application of machine learning models is streamlined and integrated, offering a complete solution for extracting meaningful insights from unstructured data.
