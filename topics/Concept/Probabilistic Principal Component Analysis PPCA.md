Probabilistic Principal Component Analysis (PPCA) is a statistical method for dimension reduction that extends classical PCA by incorporating a latent variable framework. It recovers the directions of maximal variance while also being capable of handling incomplete data. PPCA is grounded in probabilistic models, making provisions for the influence of noise and the estimation of hidden variables, thereby providing a richer modeling approach compared to traditional PCA. It is particularly useful in scenarios where data may contain missing values.
