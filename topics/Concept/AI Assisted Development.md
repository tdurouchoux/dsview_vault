A structured approach to software development that leverages AI tools to enhance productivity, code quality, and efficiency. This includes AI-assisted coding practices such as code generation, completion suggestions, refactoring, and debugging, as well as broader workflow strategies like separation of planning and execution phases, iterative annotation cycles, and systematic implementation. The goal is to minimize wasted effort, maintain control over architectural decisions, and produce high-quality results with the aid of AI-driven automation.
