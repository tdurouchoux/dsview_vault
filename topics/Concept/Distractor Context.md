Distractor context refers to irrelevant but topically related information included in a dataset to test the ability of AI systems to focus on task-relevant information and disregard extraneous details. In the context of document editing benchmarks, distractor context may include unrelated documents or data that could potentially mislead the AI system. Evaluating an AI's performance in the presence of distractors is critical for assessing its robustness and reliability in real-world scenarios.
