PLSCAN (Persistent Multiscale Density-based Clustering) is a density-based clustering algorithm introduced in the paper by D.M. Bot, L. McInnes, and J. Aerts. It serves as the foundational algorithm for the clustering extraction process in EVōC. PLSCAN is designed to handle multiscale clustering, where clusters can exist at different densities and scales. The algorithm is particularly effective for clustering embedding vectors and forms the basis for EVōC's fast and flexible clustering capabilities.
