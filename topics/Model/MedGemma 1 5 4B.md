MedGemma 1.5 4B is an updated version of the MedGemma model, designed for medical imaging and text interpretation. It supports high-dimensional medical imaging such as CT, MRI, and histopathology, as well as longitudinal medical imaging and anatomical localization. The model improves accuracy over its predecessor, MedGemma 1 4B, in various medical imaging and text-based tasks. It is intended for developers to adapt for specific medical use cases and is available for research and commercial use.
