The Transformer is a neural network architecture designed for sequence-to-sequence tasks, primarily utilized in natural language processing. It operates entirely on self-attention mechanisms rather than recurrent layers, allowing for significantly improved parallelization and efficiency in training. Transformers are capable of modeling long-range dependencies through attention mechanisms, offering versatility across tasks such as translation, summarization, and constituency parsing.
