Ministral 3 is a series of models within the Mistral 3 family, designed for edge and local use cases. It includes three model sizes: 3B, 8B, and 14B parameters. Each model size has base, instruct, and reasoning variants, all with image understanding capabilities. These models are optimized for cost-to-performance ratio and are available under the Apache 2.0 license.
