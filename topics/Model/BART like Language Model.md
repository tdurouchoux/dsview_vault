A BART-like language model is an architecture in natural language processing that combines bidirectional and autoregressive transformers to generate text. It is particularly effective for tasks such as text generation, summarization, and dialogue systems. This model is pre-trained on a large corpus and then fine-tuned on specific tasks, enabling it to understand context and generate coherent text that fits the requirements of diverse applications.
