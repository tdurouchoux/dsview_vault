DeepSeek-R1 is a recent language model designed to enhance reasoning capabilities through a structured training methodology. It focuses on fine-tuning the generation of reasoning outputs by implementing supervised fine-tuning (SFT), preference tuning, and leveraging reinforcement learning, especially in the context of recognizing and solving complex logical problems. DeepSeek-R1 is notable for its use of a precursor model specialized in reasoning and its innovative approach to large-scale reinforcement learning.
