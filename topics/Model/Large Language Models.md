A class of AI models that generate human-like text based on the input they receive, often used for various applications including chatbots. Large language models are massive pre-trained models that have transformed the field of natural language processing, enabling state-of-the-art performance across various tasks. This includes frontier models evaluated on the MLGym benchmarks, such as Claude-3.5-Sonnet and GPT-4o.
