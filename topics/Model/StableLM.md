StableLM is a series of language models developed by Stability AI, designed for various natural language processing tasks. These models include various parameter scales (3B, 7B, etc.) and architectural improvements, such as using higher-quality data for fine-tuning and employing advanced techniques like SwiGLU. StableLM models aim to achieve state-of-the-art performance for open-source language models, trained on extensive datasets and optimized for tasks like text generation, comprehension, and more.
