microGPT is a minimalistic implementation of a Generative Pre-trained Transformer (GPT) model designed for educational and research purposes. It is intentionally small and simple to enable end-to-end study, yet complex enough to include core components of modern generative models such as matrix-vector projections, self-attention mechanisms, softmax-like behavior, residual paths, normalization, and stochastic sampling. This model is trained on character-level tasks, such as generating names one character at a time, making it suitable for hardware acceleration studies and learning the intricacies of transformer architectures.
