MedASR is an open automated speech recognition (ASR) model fine-tuned for medical dictation. It is designed to convert medical speech to text accurately, making it suitable for healthcare applications. MedASR has shown significant improvements in reducing word error rates compared to generalist ASR models, particularly in medical contexts. It can be used in conjunction with MedGemma for advanced reasoning tasks and is available on Hugging Face and Vertex AI.
