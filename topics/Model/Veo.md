Veo is a state-of-the-art video generation model capable of creating high-quality video content based on textual prompts. It emphasizes realism, fidelity, and the ability to incorporate audio natively, making it a versatile tool for filmmakers and content creators. Veo 2 builds upon this model with advanced understanding of cinematic techniques, including real-world physics and human movement, supporting high-resolution outputs and various cinematic prompts to tailor the generated videos to user specifications.
