Gemini is a series of AI models from Google, designed to enhance reasoning and multimodal capabilities. Gemini 3 is the latest version, outperforming previous models in various benchmarks, including reasoning, multimodality, and coding. Gemini 2.5 builds upon the capabilities of Gemini 2.0, incorporating improvements that enhance its performance in tasks such as diagnostic accuracy and management plan appropriateness. The Gemini models are available across several Google products and are noted for their advanced reasoning and tool use capabilities.
