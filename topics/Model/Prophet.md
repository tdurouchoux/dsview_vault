Prophet is a forecasting tool developed by Facebook specifically designed for business time series data. It uses an additive model that incorporates components for trend (g(t)), seasonality (s(t)), and holidays (h(t)) to capture the underlying patterns in the data. Prophet is particularly user-friendly and automates many aspects of model tuning, making it accessible to non-experts. It is well-suited for predicting time series data that exhibits seasonal effects and trends, especially in business applications.
