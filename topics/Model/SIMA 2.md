SIMA 2 is an advanced AI agent developed by Google DeepMind, designed to operate in 3D virtual worlds. It integrates the capabilities of the Gemini models to perform complex reasoning, understand user instructions, and execute tasks in interactive environments. SIMA 2 can follow instructions, converse with users, and improve its performance over time, making it a significant step towards Artificial General Intelligence (AGI).
