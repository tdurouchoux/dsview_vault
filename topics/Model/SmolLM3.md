SmolLM3 is a state-of-the-art small language model designed for multilingual applications and capable of long-context reasoning. With 3 billion parameters, it outperforms various models at similar scales while maintaining efficiency and competitive performance with larger alternatives. SmolLM3 supports a dual instruction mode, allowing for reasoning and instruction-following, and has been optimized for specific language capabilities and long-context usage.
