SmolVLM (Small Vision Language Model) and SmolVLM2 are a series of lightweight multimodal AI models designed for efficient processing of visual and textual data, as well as video analysis. They focus on maintaining high performance while dramatically reducing parameter counts, with sizes ranging from 256 million to 2.2 billion parameters. These models are intended for various applications, including image captioning, document Q&A, visual reasoning, and advanced video understanding, demonstrating the ability to perform complex multimodal tasks with minimal computational resources.
