RealMLP is a deep learning model designed for tabular data, implemented in libraries like pytabkit. It is a multi-layer perceptron (MLP)-based approach that leverages optimized hyperparameters and automated preprocessing to achieve competitive performance. RealMLP is particularly noted for its ease of use, fast training times, and strong performance in head-to-head comparisons against boosting methods like XGBoost.
