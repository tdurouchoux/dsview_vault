VibeVoice-ASR is a unified Automatic Speech Recognition (ASR) model designed to process long-form audio (up to 60 minutes) in a single pass. It generates structured transcriptions that include speaker identification ('Who'), timestamps ('When'), and content ('What'). The model supports multilingual capabilities (over 50 languages) and allows users to incorporate customized hotwords to improve accuracy in domain-specific contexts. It leverages continuous speech tokenizers operating at a low frame rate (7.5 Hz) and employs a next-token diffusion framework for efficient processing.
