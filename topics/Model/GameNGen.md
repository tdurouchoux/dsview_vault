GameNGen is a generative model designed to simulate complex video games in real-time. It leverages neural networks and diffusion models to predict the next frame of a game based on input sequences of actions and previous frames, enabling the generation of interactive gaming environments. GameNGen demonstrates the ability to maintain high visual quality and stability over extended play sessions, making it a significant advancement in the realm of game simulation and generative modeling.
