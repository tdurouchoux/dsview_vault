Llama is a series of large language models developed by Meta, including iterations like Llama 4, optimized for various NLP tasks. The Llama models are known for their ability to handle inquiries, engage in dialogue, and generate text based on input, with recent iterations focusing on improved performance, efficiency, and customizable features through fine-tuning and integration capabilities.
