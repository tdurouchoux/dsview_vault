Gemini Robotics is an advanced vision-language-action (VLA) AI model designed for robotics applications. It enables robots to interpret and respond to complex visual and textual inputs while interacting with their physical environment. The model integrates multimodal reasoning to allow robots to perform a variety of real-world tasks with enhanced generality, interactivity, and dexterity.
