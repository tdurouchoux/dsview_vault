VibeVoice-TTS is a long-form multi-speaker Text-to-Speech (TTS) model capable of synthesizing speech up to 90 minutes in a single pass. It supports up to four distinct speakers in a single conversation, maintaining natural turn-taking and speaker consistency. The model is designed to generate expressive, natural-sounding speech with emotional nuances and supports multiple languages, including English and Chinese. It also enables cross-lingual speech synthesis.
