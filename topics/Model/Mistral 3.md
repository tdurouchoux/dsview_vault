Mistral 3 is a family of open-source AI models developed by Mistral AI. It includes several models such as Mistral Large 3, Mistral-7B and smaller models like 14B, 8B, and 3B. These models are designed to be multimodal, multilingual, and highly efficient, with capabilities in text and image understanding. They are released under the Apache 2.0 license, making them accessible for a wide range of applications and customizations.
