Mistral Large 3 is a state-of-the-art open model developed by Mistral AI. It is a sparse mixture-of-experts model trained with 41B active and 675B total parameters. It is designed to be highly capable in text and image understanding, as well as multilingual conversations. It is one of the best permissive open weight models in the world and is available under the Apache 2.0 license.
