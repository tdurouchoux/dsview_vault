MetricFlow is a core component of the dbt Semantic Layer that facilitates the definition, management, and querying of business metrics. It allows data teams to centrally define metrics within their dbt projects, ensuring consistency and reusability. MetricFlow automates the process of joining data and generating metric queries, reducing the need for manual coding. It supports the deployment of metrics and integrates seamlessly with downstream tools for consistent and reliable data access.
