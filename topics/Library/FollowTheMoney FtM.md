FollowTheMoney (FtM) is a data model and library specifically designed for anti-corruption investigations. It provides a unified framework to define, generate, validate, and export entities related to investigations, such as people and organizations. The tool can be utilized in various programming environments, such as Python and TypeScript/JavaScript, and helps in organizing and representing complex data relationships, which is crucial in the exploration of corruption cases.
