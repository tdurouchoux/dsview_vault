LangGraph is a framework for AI agents that combines natural language processing and graph-based methodologies, and serves as a modular orchestration framework for building complex, agent-driven workflows in AI applications. It enables the modeling, manipulation of complex relationships in data, enhancing the decision-making processes of agents, and supports the creation, management, and deployment of state-managed agent architectures for collaborative functionalities across various domains.
