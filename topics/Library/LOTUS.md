LOTUS is a framework designed for AI-powered data processing, enabling users to process both structured and unstructured datasets with Large Language Models (LLMs) and embeddings. It offers a Pandas-like API, providing intuitive and efficient data manipulation capabilities. LOTUS is optimized for speed, offering up to 1000x speedups, and ensures accuracy through robust algorithms. It introduces semantic operators that extend the relational model to unstructured data, supporting a wide range of data-intensive AI programs, from simple RAG to complex research-based synthesis.
