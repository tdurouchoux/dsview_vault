BERTScore is a metric that evaluates text generation by leveraging the pre-trained contextual embeddings from the BERT model. It measures the semantic similarity between generated summaries and reference summaries by calculating cosine similarity scores on word embeddings, making it more effective than traditional metrics that rely solely on n-gram overlap. BERTScore provides a more nuanced understanding of text similarity and performance in NLP tasks.
