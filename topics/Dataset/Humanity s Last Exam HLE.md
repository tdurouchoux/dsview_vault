Humanity's Last Exam (HLE) is a multi-modal benchmark dataset designed to evaluate the capabilities of large language models (LLMs). Comprising 2,500 challenging questions spanning various subjects, the dataset's primary goal is to assess advanced academic capabilities and fill the gaps left by existing benchmarks that have become too easy. The questions originated from a global community of nearly 1,000 subject matter experts from over 500 institutions.
