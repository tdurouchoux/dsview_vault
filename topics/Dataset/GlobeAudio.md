GlobeAudio is a multilingual audio dataset containing speech samples in various languages, including Russian and Chinese. The dataset is designed to evaluate models on tasks such as transcribing speech, answering questions about the content, and identifying specific information like the last word in an audio clip or the speaker's profession. GlobeAudio is used to test the robustness and accuracy of models like Inkling in handling diverse linguistic inputs and reasoning across different languages.
