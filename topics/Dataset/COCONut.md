COCONut is a large-scale human-verified dataset designed specifically for segmentation tasks in machine learning, featuring exquisite annotations that are crucial for developing and evaluating segmentation models. It includes different splits, such as COCONut-S, COCONut-B, and COCONut-L, which vary in size and complexity, making it a versatile resource for researchers in the field of computer vision, specifically segmentation.
