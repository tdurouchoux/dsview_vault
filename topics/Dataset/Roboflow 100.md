Roboflow 100 is an object detection benchmark that reviews and measures the performance of computer vision models across a wide range of imagery domains and tasks. It consists of a collection of 100 projects derived from over 90,000 public datasets and includes 224,714 images with 829 class labels, emphasizing generalization across diverse application areas prevalent in current research.
