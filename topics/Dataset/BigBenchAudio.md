BigBenchAudio is a dataset designed for evaluating the audio reasoning capabilities of language models. It includes logical statements, questions involving formal fallacies, and object-counting tasks based on audio inputs. The dataset is used to assess how well models can transcribe, interpret, and reason about spoken language, including multilingual examples. In the context of Inkling, BigBenchAudio is used to evaluate the model's performance on audio-based reasoning tasks, such as identifying logical fallacies or counting objects mentioned in audio clips.
