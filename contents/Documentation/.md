## Summary

The content provides an overview of various resources and initiatives related to digital health and artificial intelligence in healthcare. Key elements include:

- The operational framework and strategic roadmap for digital health from 2023 to 2027, highlighting public consultations on the secondary use of health data.
- Information on e-Satis, a system for evaluating patient satisfaction in hospitals through online questionnaires.
- Links to European and national regulations regarding artificial intelligence, including the EU AI Act.
- Various initiatives and projects aimed at improving digital health tools and integrating AI in diagnostics and care.
- Collaboration between health authorities and research institutions to foster innovation in digital medical devices and applications.

Additionally, several links to reports, guidelines, and strategic documents are provided for further exploration of these topics.
