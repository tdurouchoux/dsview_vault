## Summary

Mind Maps in NotebookLM visually summarize uploaded sources, presenting main topics and related ideas in a branching diagram format. They are useful for:

- Understanding the overall structure and key themes of source material.
- Exploring unfamiliar topics by providing a clear entry point to main concepts.
- Identifying connections between ideas through visual representation.
- Building a structured model to enhance comprehension and retention.

To generate a Mind Map, open or create a notebook, upload sources, and click the “Mind Map” chip in the chat. You can interact with the Mind Map by zooming, expanding/collapsing branches, and clicking nodes to ask questions. Sharing options include sharing the entire notebook or downloading the Mind Map for distribution. Users are advised to verify the accuracy of NotebookLM's responses.
