## Summary

Mind Maps in NotebookLM provide a visual summary of uploaded sources, illustrating main topics and their related ideas in a branching diagram format. 

Key uses include:
- Understanding the overall structure and key themes of source material.
- Exploring unfamiliar topics by presenting main concepts clearly.
- Identifying connections between ideas that may not be immediately apparent.
- Structuring information to enhance comprehension and retention.

To generate a Mind Map, users can open or create a notebook, click on the “Mind Map” option, and access the generated map in the Studio Panel. Interaction features include zooming, expanding/collapsing branches, and asking questions about specific nodes.

Sharing options consist of sharing the entire notebook or downloading the Mind Map for external sharing. Users are advised to verify the accuracy of NotebookLM's outputs.
