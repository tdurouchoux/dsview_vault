## Summary

The TPC-DS Benchmark models a decision support system's various aspects, including query performance and data maintenance, providing relevant and objective performance data for industry users. With its recent update to Version 3.0, the Price/Performance metric has been adjusted to better reflect current technology trends, using a multiplier of 1,000 for the metric. TPC-DS evaluates performance through query response times (single and multi-user) and data maintenance under a complex workload, making it suitable for modern systems, including Big Data. The benchmark results and specifications are accessible on the TPC website, which also offers advanced sorting options for results comparison.
