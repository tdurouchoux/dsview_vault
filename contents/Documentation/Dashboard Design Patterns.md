## Summary

The content focuses on dashboard design patterns, emphasizing their importance in simplifying complex data visualization. It discusses how dashboards enable users to efficiently view critical information across various fields such as business and healthcare. Despite existing general guidelines on dashboard design, the need for effective and practical design approaches is highlighted, especially for non-experts. The publication by multiple researchers serves as the foundation for these design patterns, aimed at enhancing creativity and streamlining the design process. An online workshop was also conducted in March 2022 to assist users in applying these design patterns. Key topics include the definition of dashboards, the rationale for design patterns, and the resources available for better dashboard design practices.
