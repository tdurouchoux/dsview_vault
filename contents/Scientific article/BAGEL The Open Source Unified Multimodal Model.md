## Summary

BAGEL is an open-source unified multimodal model designed to integrate and process data from various modalities, such as text, images, and audio. Key features include:

- **Multimodal Capabilities**: BAGEL facilitates simultaneous handling of different types of data, improving the versatility of applications.
- **Unified Architecture**: The model employs a single architecture, minimizing the need for multiple separate models and simplifying implementation.
- **Open Source**: Being open-source encourages community collaboration and innovation, allowing practitioners to modify and enhance the model.
- **Performance**: BAGEL demonstrates competitive performance in multimodal tasks, providing a robust option for developers and researchers.

Overall, BAGEL presents a significant advancement in the ability to handle multimodal data through an integrated and accessible approach.
