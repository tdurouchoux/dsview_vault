## Summary

The paper surveys the evolution and impact of knowledge graphs (KGs) in natural language processing (NLP) over the past decade. It highlights the growing interest in KGs within both academia and industry, emphasizing their role in representing semantic relationships between entities. The authors systematically analyzed 507 research papers to categorize established topics and assess the maturity of various research streams related to KGs in NLP. The survey provides a structured overview of the research landscape, a taxonomy of tasks, and summarizes key findings while suggesting future research directions.
