## Summary

The survey "A Decade of Knowledge Graphs in Natural Language Processing" analyzes the integration of knowledge graphs (KGs) in NLP, highlighting their increasing relevance in AI research. It reviews 507 papers to provide a comprehensive overview of KG applications in NLP, categorizing tasks and research types, and evaluating the maturity of various research streams. The study offers a structured landscape of the field and suggests future research directions. Main takeaways include the growing adoption of KGs in NLP and the need for systematic studies to understand the landscape better.
