## Summary

The paper surveys the role of knowledge graphs (KGs) in natural language processing (NLP) over the past decade, highlighting their increasing relevance in AI research. It systematically analyzes 507 papers to categorize established topics and assess the maturity of various research streams related to KGs in NLP. The survey provides a structured overview of the research landscape, a taxonomy of tasks, and summarizes key findings while suggesting future research directions. The study aims to fill the gap in comprehensive reviews of KGs in NLP, reflecting their rapid adoption and diverse applications in the field.
