## Summary

Mini-Gemini is a new framework designed to improve multi-modality Vision Language Models (VLMs). Despite advancements in existing VLMs for visual dialog and reasoning, a performance gap remains when compared to advanced models like GPT-4 and Gemini. Mini-Gemini aims to bridge this gap by enhancing three key areas: high-resolution visual tokens, high-quality datasets, and VLM-guided generation.

Key technical points include:
- Utilization of an additional visual encoder for high-resolution refinement, which does not increase the number of visual tokens.
- Construction of a high-quality dataset to improve image comprehension and reasoning-based generation.
- Employing dual vision encoders for providing both low-resolution embeddings and high-resolution candidates, using patch-level mining to enhance interactions between visual data and text.

Mini-Gemini is compatible with dense and Mixture of Experts (MoE) Large Language Models (LLMs) ranging from 2B to 34B parameters and demonstrates leading performance on several zero-shot benchmarks, outperforming some private models.
