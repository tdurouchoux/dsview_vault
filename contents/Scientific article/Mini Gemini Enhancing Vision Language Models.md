## Summary

Mini-Gemini is a framework designed to enhance multi-modality Vision Language Models (VLMs) by addressing performance gaps compared to advanced models like GPT-4 and Gemini. The framework focuses on three key areas: 

1. **High-Resolution Visual Tokens**: It employs an additional visual encoder to refine visual tokens without increasing their count.
2. **High-Quality Data**: A new dataset is constructed to improve image comprehension and reasoning capabilities.
3. **VLM-Guided Generation**: The integration of VLMs facilitates simultaneous image understanding, reasoning, and generation.

Mini-Gemini supports various dense and Mixture of Experts (MoE) Large Language Models (LLMs) ranging from 2B to 34B parameters and has shown leading performance in several zero-shot benchmarks, outperforming some private models. The framework utilizes dual vision encoders for low and high-resolution embeddings and employs patch-level mining to enhance visual queries.
