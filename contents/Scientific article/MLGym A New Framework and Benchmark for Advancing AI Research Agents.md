## Summary

The paper introduces Meta MLGym and MLGym-Bench, a new framework and benchmark designed for evaluating and developing large language model (LLM) agents in AI research tasks. It is the first Gym environment tailored for machine learning tasks, facilitating research on reinforcement learning algorithms. MLGym-Bench includes 13 diverse AI research tasks across various domains, requiring skills such as hypothesis generation, data processing, model training, and experimental analysis.

The authors evaluate several leading LLMs, including Claude-3.5-Sonnet and GPT-4o, finding that while these models can optimize hyperparameters and improve on existing baselines, they do not generate novel hypotheses or significant advancements in algorithms or architectures. The framework is open-sourced to encourage further research in enhancing the capabilities of LLM agents in AI research.
