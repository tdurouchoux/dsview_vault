## Summary

MLGym introduces a new framework and benchmark designed for evaluating and developing large language model (LLM) agents specifically on AI research tasks. This marks the first Gym environment tailored for machine learning tasks, enabling research on reinforcement learning algorithms. MLGym-bench encompasses 13 diverse AI research tasks across fields like computer vision, natural language processing, and game theory, requiring skills such as data generation, model training, and result analysis.

The framework allows easy integration of new tasks and models, along with synthetic data generation. Initial evaluations of several frontier LLMs indicate that while these models can enhance baseline performance by optimizing hyperparameters, they fail to produce novel hypotheses or significant advancements. The framework is open-sourced to stimulate further research in enhancing the AI capabilities of LLM agents.
