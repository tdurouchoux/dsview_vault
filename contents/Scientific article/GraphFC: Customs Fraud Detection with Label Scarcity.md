## Summary

The paper presents GraphFC, a semi-supervised graph neural network designed for customs fraud detection, addressing the challenge of limited labeled data in real-world scenarios. With the increasing volume of customs transactions and the associated risk of fraud, traditional machine learning methods are inadequate due to their reliance on extensive labeled datasets. GraphFC demonstrates significant improvements, achieving up to a 252% increase in recall compared to existing state-of-the-art methods. The model's effectiveness is validated through extensive experiments on real customs data from three different countries, showcasing its robust performance in detecting fraudulent activities.
