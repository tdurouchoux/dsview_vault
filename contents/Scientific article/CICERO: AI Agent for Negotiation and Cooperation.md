## Summary

CICERO is an AI agent developed to negotiate, persuade, and cooperate with humans, achieving human-level performance in the strategy game Diplomacy. It outperformed human players significantly, ranking in the top 10% on webDiplomacy.net. The challenge of Diplomacy lies in understanding human motivations and using natural language effectively, which CICERO excels at, often being preferred by human players for collaboration.

The development of CICERO combines strategic reasoning and natural language processing. It utilizes a controllable dialogue model based on a fine-tuned BART-like language model and a strategic reasoning engine. CICERO predicts player actions and generates contextually relevant dialogue to propose cooperative strategies.

Key techniques include:
- Iterative planning to balance dialogue consistency with rational decision-making.
- A filtering mechanism to ensure dialogue quality and strategic soundness.
- The use of a planning algorithm (piKL) to improve predictions and policies based on human behavior.

While CICERO shows promise, it still faces challenges, such as generating inconsistent dialogue. The project aims to advance human-AI interaction and has potential applications beyond gaming, such as enhancing AI communication in various domains. The code has been open-sourced to encourage further research and development in this area.
