## Summary

The manuscript provides a comprehensive overview of the field of reinforcement learning (RL), particularly focusing on deep reinforcement learning and sequential decision-making. Key topics covered include:

- Value-based reinforcement learning
- Policy-gradient methods
- Model-based methods
- A brief discussion on the integration of reinforcement learning with large language models (RL+LLMs)

The paper aims to present an up-to-date perspective on these methodologies and their applications within artificial intelligence and machine learning.
