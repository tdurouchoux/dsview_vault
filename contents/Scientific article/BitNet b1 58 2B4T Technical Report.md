## Summary

BitNet b1.58 2B4T is introduced as the first open-source, native 1-bit Large Language Model (LLM) with 2 billion parameters, trained on a corpus of 4 trillion tokens. The model has been evaluated across various benchmarks, demonstrating competitive performance with leading full-precision LLMs of similar size. Key advantages include reduced memory usage, lower energy consumption, and decreased decoding latency, making it more computationally efficient. The model weights and open-source inference implementations are available via Hugging Face, supporting further research and adoption.
