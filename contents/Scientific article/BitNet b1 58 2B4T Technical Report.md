## Summary

BitNet b1.58 2B4T is the first open-source, native 1-bit Large Language Model (LLM) with 2 billion parameters, trained on a dataset of 4 trillion tokens. It has been evaluated on various benchmarks, including language understanding, mathematical reasoning, coding, and conversational skills. The model demonstrates performance comparable to leading full-precision LLMs of similar size while offering advantages in computational efficiency, such as reduced memory usage, lower energy consumption, and faster decoding times. The model weights and open-source inference implementations for both GPU and CPU are available on Hugging Face to encourage further research and adoption.
