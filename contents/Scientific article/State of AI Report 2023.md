## Summary

The State of AI Report 2023, produced by Nathan Benaich and the Air Street Capital team, reviews significant developments in AI across several dimensions: Research, Industry, Politics, Safety, and Predictions. Key themes include:

- GPT-4's dominance over other LLMs, showcasing the effectiveness of proprietary architectures and reinforcement learning.
- Increased efforts to replicate or exceed proprietary AI performance through smaller models and improved datasets, driven by concerns over the sustainability of human-generated data.
- Advancements in life sciences facilitated by LLMs and diffusion models, particularly in molecular biology and drug discovery.
- The rising importance of compute resources, with NVIDIA achieving record earnings and the U.S. implementing trade restrictions affecting chip exports.
- A surge in venture capital funding for generative AI startups, raising over $18 billion despite a downturn in tech valuations.
- A growing safety debate leading to varied governmental responses, highlighting divisions within the AI community and challenges in establishing global governance.
- Difficulties in evaluating state-of-the-art models, as standard LLMs often lack robustness, indicating that current evaluation methods may be insufficient.
