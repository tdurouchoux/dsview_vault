## Summary

The MIT HAN Lab offers a course titled "TinyML and Efficient Deep Learning Computing" (6.5940) for Fall 2024, focusing on efficient AI computing technologies. The course aims to explore advancements in deep learning and their applications in TinyML, which facilitates machine learning on resource-limited devices. For more details, the course materials and further information are accessible via the course's homepage.
