## Summary

Stanford's "Introduction to Statistics" on Coursera is a beginner-friendly course covering foundational statistical concepts. It consists of 12 modules, including Descriptive Statistics, Sampling, Probability, Regression, Confidence Intervals, Hypothesis Testing, ANOVA, and Resampling. Key skills gained include statistical analysis, regression, data visualization, and hypothesis testing. The course emphasizes exploratory data analysis, sampling principles, and significance tests. Taught by Guenther Walther, it offers flexible learning with video lectures, assignments, and quizzes. Learners receive a shareable certificate upon completion. Reviews highlight its thorough overview but note some topics require supplementary resources for deeper understanding. Financial aid is available.
