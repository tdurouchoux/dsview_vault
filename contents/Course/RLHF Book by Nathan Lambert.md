## Summary

The RLHF Book by Nathan Lambert is a short course introducing Reinforcement Learning from Human Feedback (RLHF) for language models. It includes lectures, talks, and slides, structured into core sections:

**Lectures:**
1. **Overview (Ch. 1-3):** Foundations of RLHF and post-training.
2. **IFT, Reward Models & Rejection Sampling (Ch. 4, 5, 9):** Core optimization methods.
3. **RL Motivation & Math (Ch. 6, Part 1):** Policy gradients theory and intuition.
4. **RL Implementation & Practice (Ch. 6, Part 2):** Code, loss aggregation, async training, and engineering.

**Other Lectures:** Includes invited talks (e.g., SALA 2026).

The book is available online ([rlhfbook.com](https://rlhfbook.com)) and includes practical resources like PDFs, slides, and code. Citation format provided.
