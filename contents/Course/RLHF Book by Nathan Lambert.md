## Summary

The book "Reinforcement Learning from Human Feedback" by Nathan Lambert provides an introduction to RLHF, particularly in the context of language models. It details its origins across various disciplines such as economics and philosophy. Key topics include:

- **Definitions and Problem Formulation:** Outlines fundamental concepts and common mathematical frameworks used in RLHF.
- **Optimization Stages:** Discusses the progression from instruction tuning, training reward models, to the utilization of rejection sampling, reinforcement learning approaches, and direct alignment algorithms.
- **Advanced Topics:** Addresses less explored areas such as synthetic data and evaluation metrics, along with open questions in the field.

The changelog indicates iterative updates and improvements to specific sections of the book. Acknowledgements recognize contributions from various individuals and GitHub collaborators who aided in the project.
