## Summary

The lecture by Yann LeCun explores the path toward achieving human-level intelligence in machines. Key points include:

- Definition of human-level intelligence and its characteristics, such as reasoning, learning, perception, and social interaction.
- The role of deep learning and neural networks in advancing machine learning capabilities.
- Discussion on the limitations of current AI systems and the need for new paradigms.
- The importance of incorporating common sense reasoning and understanding into AI models.
- Exploration of potential frameworks and methodologies that may lead to breakthroughs in AI research and development.

Overall, LeCun emphasizes a goal-oriented approach to bridge the gap between current AI capabilities and human-like intelligence.
