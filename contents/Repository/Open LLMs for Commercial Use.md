## Summary

The GitHub repository "eugeneyan/open-llms" provides a comprehensive list of open Large Language Models (LLMs) that are available for commercial use. The models are licensed under various terms such as Apache 2.0, MIT, and OpenRAIL-M, allowing for flexibility in usage and modification.

Key highlights include:
- A variety of LLMs with details on their release dates, parameters, context lengths, and associated research papers or blogs.
- Notable models include T5, GPT-NeoX-20B, Bloom, and LLaMA 2, among others, each with specific capabilities and licensing.
- The repository also categorizes models for specific applications, such as code generation and instruction tuning, along with datasets used for training and evaluation.
- Licensing explanations clarify the permissions and restrictions associated with each model, emphasizing the importance of understanding legal implications before commercial use.

Overall, this repository serves as a valuable resource for data scientists and developers looking to leverage open-source LLMs in their projects.
