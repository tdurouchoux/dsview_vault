## Summary

Mistral AI introduces Mistral 3, a next-generation open multimodal and multilingual AI model family. Key points include:

- **Models**: Mistral 3 includes small dense models (14B, 8B, 3B) and Mistral Large 3, a sparse mixture-of-experts model with 41B active and 675B total parameters, all under Apache 2.0 license.
- **Performance**: Mistral Large 3 achieves state-of-the-art performance, ranking #2 in the OSS non-reasoning models category on the LMArena leaderboard, with strong multilingual and image understanding capabilities.
- **Collaborations**: Partnered with NVIDIA, vLLM, and Red Hat for optimized deployment, including support for NVIDIA's hardware and software ecosystems.
- **Edge Use**: Ministral 3 series (3B, 8B, 14B) offers cost-effective, high-performance models for edge and local use cases, with multimodal and multilingual capabilities.
- **Availability**: Mistral 3 is available on multiple platforms, including Mistral AI Studio, Amazon Bedrock, Azure Foundry, and Hugging Face, with customization services for tailored AI solutions.
- **Key Features**: Frontier performance, multimodal and multilingual support, scalable efficiency, and adaptability for various applications.

The release emphasizes open access, transparency, and collective progress in AI development.
