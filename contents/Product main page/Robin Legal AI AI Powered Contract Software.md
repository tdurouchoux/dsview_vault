## Summary

Robin is an AI-powered platform designed for legal teams, enhancing contract management and legal intelligence. Key features include:

- Instant insights and contract finalization, streamlining the review process significantly (up to 80% faster).
- Searchable conversations about documents, promoting collaboration while keeping discussions organized.
- Advanced search functionality that understands context, detecting contract types and key terms efficiently.
- Smart alerts for deadlines and commitments, ensuring accountability and preventing missed obligations.
- A unified workspace that allows flexible management of all legal documents in a secure environment.

The platform ensures compliance with GDPR and holds certifications for ISO27001 and SOC2, prioritizing user privacy and security. Robin has processed over 500,000 documents and provides global coverage, with resources such as a help center, webinars, and educational podcasts available for users.
