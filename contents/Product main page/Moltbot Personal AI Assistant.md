## Summary

Moltbot is a personal AI assistant that operates from your own machine, offering privacy and control over your data. It can be accessed via popular chat apps like WhatsApp, Telegram, and Discord, and features persistent memory to learn and adapt to your preferences. Moltbot can browse the web, control your system, and integrate with various tools and services. It supports skills and plugins, allowing users to extend its functionality. The assistant is open-source and can be installed via a one-liner command or by cloning the GitHub repository. Moltbot has been featured in notable tech publications and is continuously updated with new features and integrations.
