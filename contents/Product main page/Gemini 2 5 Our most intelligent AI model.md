## Summary

Gemini 2.5 is Google's latest AI model, focusing on advanced reasoning and coding capabilities. The initial release, Gemini 2.5 Pro Experimental, excels in performance benchmarks, showcasing significant improvements in complex problem-solving and reasoning tasks. Key features include:

- **Enhanced Reasoning**: The model demonstrates superior analytical skills, incorporating context for informed decision-making and achieving state-of-the-art results on benchmarks like GPQA and Humanity’s Last Exam.

- **Advanced Coding**: Significant advancements in coding tasks, including the creation of web applications and code transformation, with impressive scores on industry-standard evaluations.

- **Multimodal Capabilities**: Gemini 2.5 can understand and process diverse data types (text, audio, images, and video) with an extensive context window—1 million tokens currently, expanding to 2 million soon.

Availability includes Google AI Studio for developers and Gemini Advanced users, with future integration into Vertex AI. Overall, Gemini 2.5 aims to enhance AI performance and user support in handling complex tasks efficiently.
