## Summary

MusicLM is a new experimental AI tool from Google that transforms text descriptions into music. Users can sign up to try it via AI Test Kitchen on web, Android, or iOS. By inputting prompts like “soulful jazz for a dinner party,” MusicLM generates two versions of a song, allowing users to listen and provide feedback to improve the model.

The development of MusicLM involved collaboration with musicians to gather insights on how the technology can enhance creativity. It aims to support both professional musicians and beginners in expressing their musical ideas.
