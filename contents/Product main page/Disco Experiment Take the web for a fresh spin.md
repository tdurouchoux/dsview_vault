## Summary

Disco is a platform introducing GenTabs, a web discovery feature that transforms open browser tabs into custom, interactive apps. These GenTabs are tailored to users' specific tabs and goals, offering versatile applications such as exploring the solar system, planning trips, creating meal plans, and organizing gardens. The platform is currently inviting users to join a waitlist for early access to new tools and local events. Disco is part of a broader ecosystem that includes Google AI, Google Cloud, and Google Research.
