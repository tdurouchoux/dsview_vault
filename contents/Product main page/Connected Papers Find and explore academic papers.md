## Summary

Connected Papers is a tool designed to help users find and explore academic papers related to specific topics. It creates a visual graph of connections between papers, allowing researchers to easily trace the development of ideas and discover relevant literature. The platform is user-friendly and facilitates the navigation of complex academic landscapes, although it requires JavaScript to function properly.
