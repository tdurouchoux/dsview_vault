## Summary

Looker is Google Cloud's BI platform with embedded analytics, leveraging AI (Gemini) and a semantic layer (LookML) for governed, agentic BI. Key features include conversational analytics for natural language queries and automated actions, self-service BI with AI-driven insights, and seamless integration with Google Cloud (BigQuery, IAM, SSO). It supports embedded analytics for custom data apps, cross-cloud cost management, marketing analytics, and collaboration tools (e.g., Google Workspace). Pricing includes platform and user-based tiers (Standard, Enterprise, Embed). Ideal for scalable, secure, and AI-powered data-driven decision-making.
