## Summary

Connected Papers is a tool designed to help users find and explore academic papers. It allows researchers to visualize the relationships between different papers, facilitating the discovery of relevant literature in a specific field. The platform creates a graph of connected papers based on their citations and references, making it easier to track the evolution of research topics. Users can interact with the graph to delve deeper into specific papers and their connections. Note that JavaScript must be enabled for the tool to function properly.
