## Summary

SAM Audio is a tool designed for audio processing and analysis. It offers a range of features for data scientists working with audio data. Key functionalities include:

- **Audio Segmentation**: Automatically segments audio files into meaningful parts.
- **Feature Extraction**: Extracts various audio features such as pitch, tempo, and spectral characteristics.
- **Noise Reduction**: Provides algorithms to reduce background noise from audio recordings.
- **Speech Recognition**: Integrates speech-to-text capabilities for transcribing spoken content.
- **Visualization**: Offers tools to visualize audio data, including spectrograms and waveform plots.

The tool is designed to be user-friendly, with a focus on ease of use and integration into existing data science workflows. It supports multiple audio formats and can be used for a variety of applications, from music analysis to speech processing.
