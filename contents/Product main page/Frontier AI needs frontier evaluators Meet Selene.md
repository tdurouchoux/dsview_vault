## Summary

Atla introduces Selene 1, a top-tier LLM Judge designed for evaluating generative AI outputs. It surpasses models from leading labs, including OpenAI and Anthropic, on 11 evaluation benchmarks. Selene excels in various evaluation tasks, such as absolute scoring, classification, and pairwise preference assessments, providing detailed critiques for its evaluations.

Key features include:
- Fine-grained steering capabilities, enabling customization of evaluation criteria.
- Introduction of Eval Copilot, a tool for users to generate and refine evaluation metrics with minimal prompt engineering.
- Strong performance on benchmarks like FLASK, Auto-J, and RewardBench, showcasing Selene's ability to align with human preferences.

Selene integrates easily into existing workflows via API, making it accessible and adaptable for different evaluation needs. The Eval Copilot enhances its usability by allowing users to adjust prompts and scoring rubrics efficiently.
