## Summary

Datawrapper is a web-based tool for creating and publishing high-quality data visualizations, including charts, maps, and tables. It offers a step-based workflow for guided visualization creation, with powerful customization options and a responsive preview feature. Key features include:

- **Design and Branding**: Professional, accessible, and customizable visualizations that match your brand's style.
- **Collaboration**: Real-time collaboration, comments for feedback, and shared settings for team consistency.
- **Organization**: Team folders, workflows, and separate team settings for better organization and security.
- **Embedding**: Visualizations can be embedded on various platforms, including websites, presentations, and printed publications.
- **Security**: Single-sign-on, centralized account management, and self-hosting options for compliance and security.

Datawrapper is used by numerous organizations and publications for creating data visualizations.
