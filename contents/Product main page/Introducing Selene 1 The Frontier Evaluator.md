## Summary

Atla has introduced Selene 1, a cutting-edge large language model (LLM) specifically designed for evaluating generative AI responses. Selene 1 outperforms leading models from OpenAI, Anthropic, and others across 11 evaluation benchmarks. It excels in various evaluation tasks, including absolute scoring, classification, and pairwise preference, providing actionable critiques to support its assessments.

Key features include:
- Fine-grained steering capabilities, allowing users to customize evaluation criteria.
- An Alignment Platform that enables users to generate and refine evaluation metrics with minimal prompt engineering.
- Strong performance in capturing human preferences, particularly in nuanced evaluations, achieving high correlation with human scores on complex tasks.

Selene 1 is available via API and SDK, facilitating easy integration into existing workflows. The Alignment Platform enhances usability by allowing users to adjust prompts and test custom evaluation needs effectively.
