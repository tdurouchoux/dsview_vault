## Summary

Google has introduced Gemma, a new family of lightweight, state-of-the-art open models designed for responsible AI development. Built on the same research as the Gemini models, Gemma includes two sizes: 2B and 7B, with pre-trained and instruction-tuned variants. Key features include:

- A Responsible Generative AI Toolkit to guide safe AI application development.
- Compatibility with major frameworks (JAX, PyTorch, TensorFlow) and tools like Hugging Face and NVIDIA NeMo.
- Models can run on various devices, including laptops and cloud platforms, with optimized performance on NVIDIA GPUs and Google Cloud TPUs.
- Terms of use allow responsible commercial usage for all organizations.

Gemma emphasizes safety, utilizing automated data filtering and reinforcement learning to ensure responsible outputs. Developers can access free resources, including credits for Google Cloud, to facilitate experimentation and innovation. Further details and quickstart guides are available on the Google AI website.
