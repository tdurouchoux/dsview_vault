## Summary

Rill introduces Generative Business Intelligence (GenBI), which transforms the creation of business dashboards by allowing users to describe their data needs conversationally, eliminating the need for complex interfaces or SQL queries. The foundation of GenBI is the BI-as-Code approach, where dashboards and metrics are defined as code, enabling AI to understand and generate analytics based on structured definitions.

Key aspects of GenBI include:
- **Declarative Nature**: Metrics and relationships are defined in a way that AI can comprehend, facilitating natural language querying and dashboard generation.
- **Metrics Layer**: This serves as the semantic foundation, capturing business definitions and relationships, which aids AI in generating accurate visualizations and metrics.
- **Human-AI Collaboration**: Users can interact with the system using natural language, allowing for iterative refinement of dashboards and metrics.

The evolution from traditional BI to GenBI highlights the shift towards a more accessible, code-first approach that enhances collaboration between data teams and business users. GenBI leverages AI to automate and streamline the analytics process, making it easier for non-technical users to derive insights from data.

Rill Developer exemplifies this approach by integrating GenAI capabilities, allowing users to create and modify dashboards and metrics directly in code, thus improving productivity and enabling faster insights. The future of GenBI promises democratized access to data analytics while maintaining the robustness of traditional BI systems.
