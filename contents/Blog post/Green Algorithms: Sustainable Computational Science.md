## Summary

The Green Algorithms project focuses on promoting environmentally sustainable computational science. It has received recognition for its impact on policy and tangible environmental benefits, winning the 2024 Susannah Boddie Impact of the Year Award. The project includes a carbon footprint calculator for researchers, resources for sustainable computing practices, and training materials. A recent publication outlines the GREENER principles for sustainable computational science, developed in collaboration with key stakeholders. The initiative also encourages the formation of a Community of Practice for those interested in sustainable computational practices.
