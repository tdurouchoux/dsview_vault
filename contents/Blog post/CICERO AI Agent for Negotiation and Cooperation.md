## Summary

CICERO is an AI agent developed to negotiate, persuade, and cooperate with humans, achieving human-level performance in the strategy game Diplomacy. It outperformed human players significantly, ranking in the top 10% on webDiplomacy.net. The challenge of Diplomacy lies in understanding human motivations and using natural language effectively, which CICERO excels at, often being preferred by human players for collaboration.

The development of CICERO involved integrating strategic reasoning and natural language processing. It utilizes a controllable dialogue model based on a fine-tuned BART-like language model, trained on extensive game data. CICERO generates dialogue that aligns with its strategic plans, enhancing its negotiation capabilities.

Key components of CICERO's functionality include:
1. Predicting player actions based on game state and dialogue.
2. Iteratively refining predictions using a planning algorithm called piKL, which balances dialogue consistency with rational decision-making.
3. Generating coherent and persuasive dialogue to facilitate cooperation.

Despite its advancements, CICERO still faces challenges, such as generating inconsistent dialogue. The project aims to advance human-AI interaction and has potential applications beyond gaming, such as improving AI communication in various domains. The code has been open-sourced to encourage further research and development in this area.
