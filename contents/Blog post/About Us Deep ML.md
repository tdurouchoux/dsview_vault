## Summary

Deep-ML is focused on making deep learning challenges engaging and accessible for all skill levels, from beginners to experts. Their mission is to provide accessible AI education and foster a community for continuous learning and innovation. The team consists of AI enthusiasts and tech experts dedicated to creating high-quality educational content. They encourage interaction through various channels for questions, feedback, and contributions.
