## Summary

Taalas, a 2.5-year-old startup, developed a platform to transform AI models into custom silicon, reducing deployment time to 2 months. Their Hardcore Models are 10x faster, cheaper, and lower power than software-based implementations, addressing AI's latency and cost barriers. Key principles include total specialization, merging storage and computation, and radical simplification. Their first product, a hard-wired Llama 3.1 8B, achieves 17K tokens/sec/user, 10x faster than state-of-the-art, with 20x lower cost and 10x less power. Future models include a mid-sized reasoning LLM (spring) and a frontier LLM (winter). Taalas aims to enable instantaneous AI applications with sub-millisecond latency and near-zero cost, fostering innovation in AI development.
