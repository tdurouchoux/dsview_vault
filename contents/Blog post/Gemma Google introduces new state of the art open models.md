## Summary

Google has introduced Gemma, a new family of state-of-the-art open models designed for responsible AI development. Key features include:

- **Model Sizes**: Available in two sizes—Gemma 2B and 7B—with pre-trained and instruction-tuned variants.
- **Toolkits**: Launched alongside a Responsible Generative AI Toolkit offering guidance and essential tools for safer AI application development.
- **Framework Support**: Compatible with JAX, PyTorch, and TensorFlow (Keras 3.0), with tools for inference and supervised fine-tuning.
- **Accessibility**: Models can be deployed on various platforms (laptops, desktops, Google Cloud) and are optimized for performance on NVIDIA GPUs and Google TPUs.
- **Performance**: Gemma models outperform larger models in key benchmarks, adhering to safety standards through data filtering and human feedback techniques.
- **Research Incentives**: Free access is available via Kaggle, Colab, and Google Cloud credits for researchers.

Gemma aims to empower developers with resources to innovate while maintaining a commitment to ethical AI practices.
