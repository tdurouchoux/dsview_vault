## Summary

The article discusses the concept of livable cities and their urban networks, emphasizing the importance of road networks in urban planning. It highlights various rankings of the most livable cities, with Zurich, Vienna, and Copenhagen frequently appearing at the top. The author merges six credible rankings to identify twelve cities that are considered the most livable based on multiple criteria, including public transport and healthcare.

The article also explores the use of AI, specifically ChatGPT, to generate color palettes for visualizing urban networks. The author utilizes OSMnx, a Python package, to visualize the road networks of these cities, demonstrating the application of spatial data science in urban studies. The visualizations are enhanced with AI-generated color pairings to improve contrast and clarity.

Key takeaways include the significance of urban network visualization in addressing transportation issues and the role of AI in assisting data visualization tasks. The article concludes with a gallery showcasing the visualized road networks of the identified livable cities.
