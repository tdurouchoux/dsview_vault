## Summary

US government directive suspends access to Fable 5 and Mythos 5 for all foreign nationals, citing national security concerns over potential jailbreaks. Anthropic complied but disputes the severity of the risk, arguing the disclosed jailbreak method is narrow, non-universal, and already mitigated by other models. The company emphasizes its defense-in-depth strategy and calls for transparent, technically grounded regulatory processes. Access to other models remains unaffected.
