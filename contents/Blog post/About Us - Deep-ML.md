Deep-ML is dedicated to making deep learning challenges enjoyable and accessible for all skill levels, from beginners to experts. Their mission is to provide engaging AI education and foster a community focused on continuous learning and innovation. The team consists of AI enthusiasts and tech experts committed to creating high-quality educational content. They encourage interaction through various channels for questions, feedback, and contributions.
