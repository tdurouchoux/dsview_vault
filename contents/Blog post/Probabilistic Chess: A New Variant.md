## Summary

Probabilistic Chess is a new variant of chess that introduces success probabilities for each move, making the game more strategic and unpredictable. Key features include:

- **Movement Probability**: Each square has a defined probability of successful movement, determined by a dice roll.
- **Winning Condition**: The game is won by capturing the opponent's king, not just achieving checkmate.
- **Dynamic Gameplay**: Players can make moves while in check, and self-checks are allowed, adding complexity to strategies.
- **Guaranteed Wins**: All games end in a win, eliminating draws and stalemates, which enhances excitement.
- **Variants**: Different gameplay options are being tested, such as guaranteed successful king moves to balance gameplay.
- **Implementation**: The game is built using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, with a focus on performance and AI development.
- **AI Development**: The AI uses a combination of traditional chess algorithms and probabilistic reasoning to make decisions, with ongoing improvements planned.

Overall, Probabilistic Chess aims to provide a fresh and engaging experience by blending traditional chess mechanics with elements of chance.
