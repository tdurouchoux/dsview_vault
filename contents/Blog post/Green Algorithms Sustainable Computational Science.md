## Summary

Green Algorithms is an initiative focused on promoting environmentally sustainable computational science. Key components include:

- **Carbon Footprint Calculator**: A tool for researchers to estimate the carbon footprint of their computational projects.
- **Green Algorithms 4 HPC**: A specialized calculator for assessing the carbon footprint of computations on High-Performance Computing (HPC) platforms.
- **Sustainable Computing Tips**: Resources and best practices for reducing environmental impact in computational work.

The project has received recognition, winning the 2024 Susannah Boddie Impact of the Year Award for its influence on policy and tangible environmental benefits. A recent publication outlines the GREENER principles for sustainable computational science, developed in collaboration with major stakeholders. The initiative is also forming a Community of Practice to further engage in environmentally sustainable practices in computational research.
