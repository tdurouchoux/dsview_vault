## Summary

CICERO is an AI developed to negotiate, persuade, and cooperate with humans, achieving human-level performance in the strategy game Diplomacy. By utilizing advanced techniques in strategic reasoning and natural language processing, CICERO successfully navigates social dynamics and complex planning required in the game, outperforming human players and ranking in the top 10 percent.

Key components of CICERO include:

1. **Controllable Dialogue Model**: Built on a large language model fine-tuned on data from human gameplay, allowing CICERO to generate contextually appropriate dialogue while coordinating strategies with other players.
  
2. **Dialogue-Aware Strategy**: CICERO employs a planning algorithm that incorporates human communication patterns, enabling it to balance rational gameplay with interaction dynamics.

3. **Natural Language Generation**: The AI is designed to engage in persuasive dialogue, offering strategic proposals and negotiating alliances effectively.

Despite its capabilities, CICERO can produce inconsistent dialogue, highlighting the challenges in AI-human interaction and the need for robustness in real-world applications. The open-sourcing of the CICERO code aims to encourage further research in human-AI cooperation while addressing issues related to alignment and dialogue quality. Future prospects for this technology include enhancing AI assistants for more complex, skill-teaching interactions and creating NPCs in video games that engage in meaningful conversations with players.
