## Summary

The podcast "Oops! We're Both Unemployed!!" by Jacqueline Nolis and Emily Robinson is an extension of their book "Build a Career in Data Science." It addresses various aspects of building and navigating a data science career, including job searching, project lifecycles, and management roles. Key episodes cover topics such as:

- Personal experiences with unemployment and job searching.
- Specialization in data science subfields and community involvement.
- Managing relationships with stakeholders and managers.
- Career progression options for seasoned data scientists.
- Strategies for leaving a job gracefully and negotiating job offers.
- The importance of building a portfolio and preparing for interviews.

The podcast emphasizes practical advice, real-life stories, and the emotional aspects of a data science career, aiming to fill gaps left by traditional data science education.
