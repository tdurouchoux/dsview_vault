## Summary

The content provides insights into benchmarking data teams across various metrics analyzed from tens of thousands of companies. Key areas covered include:

- **Data Team Size**: Analyzed the ratio of data team size to total company size in 100 tech scaleups.
- **Team Composition**: Examined the distribution of roles within data teams, focusing on insights, data engineering, and machine learning.
- **Data-to-Engineer Ratio**: Compared data and product team sizes relative to engineering teams in 40 scaleups.
- **Salary Benchmark**: Offered comparisons of data team salaries from hundreds of European companies.
- **Data Stack**: Identified the most popular tools used in data warehousing, business intelligence (BI), transformation, ingestion, and catalogs.

Additionally, there's a call to action to explore SYNQ's guide for building reliable data products.
