## Summary

The content provides insights from an analysis of over 100 fast-growing companies regarding data team metrics. Key areas covered include:

- **Data Team Size**: Examines the ratio of data team size to total company size in tech scaleups.
- **Team Composition**: Details the distribution of roles within data teams, including insights, data engineering, and machine learning.
- **Data-to-Engineer Ratio**: Compares the sizes of data and product teams to engineering teams in 40 scaleups.
- **Salary Benchmark**: Offers comparisons of data team salaries across various European companies.

The content encourages further exploration of building reliable data products through a guide from SYNQ.
