## Summary

The content discusses the evolution of Data Mesh architecture and its potential to enhance value in modern enterprises. Key points include:

- Data's value increases when combined across various business processes, enabling better analytics and AI/ML predictions.
- Organizations must balance data sharing with risk management, especially in regulated industries.
- JPMC's approach involves defining 'Data Products' and implementing a Data Mesh architecture to facilitate data sharing while maintaining control.
- Data products are curated collections of related data stored in separate data lakes, allowing for efficient access and management.
- The architecture supports in-place data consumption, minimizing discrepancies and ensuring data accuracy.
- A cloud-based Mesh Catalog provides visibility into data flows, enhancing oversight for data product owners.
- The Data Mesh enables streamlined reporting by allowing teams to access and audit data from various domains efficiently.

Overall, JPMC's Data Mesh architecture aligns data technology with product strategy, promoting effective data management and sharing across the enterprise.
