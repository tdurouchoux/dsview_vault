## Summary

The podcast "Oops! We're Both Unemployed!!" by Jacqueline Nolis and Emily Robinson is an extension of their book "Build a Career in Data Science." It addresses various aspects of building and navigating a career in data science, including job searching, project lifecycles, and management roles. 

Key episodes cover:
- Personal experiences with unemployment and job searching.
- Specializations within data science and the importance of community involvement.
- Managing relationships with stakeholders and managers.
- Career progression options for seasoned data scientists.
- Strategies for gracefully leaving a job and negotiating offers.
- The job interview process, including resume tips and building a portfolio.
- The skills required for data science roles and the types of companies that hire data scientists.

Overall, the podcast emphasizes practical advice, personal stories, and insights into the data science field, aiming to equip listeners with the knowledge to succeed in their careers.
